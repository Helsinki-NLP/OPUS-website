(src)="241463"> Sven Constantin Voelpel ( born October 13 , 1973 in Munich ) is a German organizational theorist and Professor of Business Administration at the School of Humanities and Social Sciences at Jacobs University in Bremen , Germany , known for his work in the field of strategic management , business models[1 ] and knowledge management.[2 ]
(trg)="8"> स्वेन कोन्स्तन्तीन वोएल्पेल ( जन्म अक्टोबर १३ , १९७३ मुनिच ( Munich ) ) एक जर्मन संगठनात्मक सिद्धान्तकर्ता र मानविकी र सामाजिक विज्ञान का  व्यापार प्रशासन मा प्रोफेस्सोर हुन जाकोब्स युनिभर्सिटी ,  ब्रेमेनमा ।  उनि विशेस्त रणनीतिक व्यवस्थापन , ब्यापार नमुना [ 1 ] र ज्ञान व्यवस्थापन मा आफ्नो काम को लागी चिनिन्छन । [ 2 ]

(src)="241465"> Voelpel received his MA in economics , social sciences , and business administration from the University of Augsburg in 1999 , and his PhD from the University of St. Gallen in Switzerland in 1993.[3 ]
(trg)="10"> वोएल्पेल ले सन १९९९ मा औग्स्बुर्ग युनिभर्सिटीबाट अर्थशास्त्र , सामाजिक विज्ञान र व्यापार प्रशासनमा एमए प्राप्त गरे । उन्ले आफ्नो पीएचडी सन १९९३ मा स्वित्जेर्लन्द को युनिभर्सिटीओफ सैन्त गल्लेन बाट हासिल गरे । [ 3 ]
(src)="241466"> Voepel started his academic career as post-graduate at Harvard University in 2003 , and continued his research at Oxford University until 2008 .
(src)="241467"> In the year 2003-2004 he was Associate Professor at the University of Groningen , and in the Norwegian School of Economics .
(src)="241468"> In 2004 he moved to the Jacobs University Bremen , where he was appointed Professor of Business Administration.[3 ]
(trg)="11"> वोएल्पेल ले आफ्नो शैक्षिक क्यारियर हार्वर्द युनिभर्सिटीमा स्नाताकोत्तर को रुप्मा २००३ साल मा सुरु गरे भने , २००८ साल सम्म ओक्स्फोर्द युनिभर्सिटीमा आफ्नो अनुसन्धान जारी गरे । सन २००३-४ मा उनि युनिभर्सिटी ओफ ग्रोनिङेन , र णोर्वेगिअन स्कुल ओफ इकोनोमिक्स  मा थिए ।  २००४ मा उनि जकोब्स युनिभर्सिटी ब्रेमेन , मा आए जहाँ उनि व्यापार प्रशासन को प्रोफेस्सोर को रुप्मा नियुक्त भए । [ 3 ]
(src)="241469"> In 2007 , Voelpel founded the WDN - WISE Demographics Network in Bremen ( Germany ) and has been the director ever since .
(src)="241470"> The WDN provides specific and scientific solutions to demography-related personnel issues in its partner companies .

(src)="241471"> Voelpel has served as the editorial board member for the Journal of Change Management ( 2004-2010 ) , the Journal of Knowledge Management ( 2004-2006 ) , and has been the member of editorial board for Organization Studies ( Journal ) since 2008 .

(src)="241847"> Newton 's law of universal gravitation states that a particle attracts every other particle in the universe using a force that is directly proportional to the product of their masses but also inversely proportional to the square of the distance between them.[note 1 ] This is a general physical law derived from empirical observations by what Isaac Newton called induction.[1 ] It is a part of classical mechanics and was formulated in Newton 's work Philosophiæ Naturalis Principia Mathematica ( " the Principia " ) , first published on 5 July 1687 .
(src)="241848"> ( When Newton 's book was presented in 1686 to the Royal Society , Robert Hooke made a claim that Newton had obtained the inverse square law from him ; see the History section below . )
