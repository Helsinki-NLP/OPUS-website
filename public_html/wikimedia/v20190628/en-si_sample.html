(src)="334797"> Ambient backscatter uses existing radio frequency signals , such as radio , television and digital telephony , to transmit data without a battery or power grid connection .

(src)="334799"> Antennas on other devices , in turn , detect that signal and can respond accordingly .

(src)="335223"> The Great Gatsby is a 1925 novel written by American author F. Scott Fitzgerald that follows a cast of characters living in the fictional town of West Egg on prosperous Long Island in the summer of 1922 .
(src)="335224"> The story primarily concerns the young and mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and his quixotic passion and obsession for the beautiful former debutante Daisy Buchanan .
(src)="335225"> Considered to be Fitzgerald 's magnum opus , The Great Gatsby explores themes of decadence , idealism , resistance to change , social upheaval , and excess , creating a portrait of the Jazz Age or the Roaring Twenties that has been described as a cautionary tale regarding the American Dream.[1][2 ]

(src)="335226"> Fitzgerald—inspired by the parties he had attended while visiting Long Island 's north shore—began planning the novel in 1923 , desiring to produce , in his words , " something new—something extraordinary and beautiful and simple and intricately patterned."[3 ] Progress was slow , with Fitzgerald completing his first draft following a move to the French Riviera in 1924 .
