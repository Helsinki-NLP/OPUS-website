(trg)="729"> Sobhuza II , KBE ( also known as Nkhotfotjeni,[1 ] Mona[2][3 ] ) ( 22 July 1899  – 21 August 1982 ) was the Paramount Chief and later King of Swaziland for 82 years , the longest verifiable reign of any monarch in recorded history .
(trg)="730"> Sobhuza was born on 22 July 1899 at Zombodze Royal Residence , the son of Inkhosikati Lomawa Ndwandwe and King Ngwane V. When he was only four months old , his father died suddenly while dancing incwala .
(trg)="731"> Sobhuza was chosen King soon after that and his grandmother Labotsibeni and his uncle Prince Malunge led the Swazi nation until his maturity in 1921.[4 ] Sobhuza led Swaziland through independence until his death in 1982 .
(trg)="732"> He was succeeded by Mswati III , his young son with Inkhosikati Ntfombi Tfwala , who was crowned in 1986 .
