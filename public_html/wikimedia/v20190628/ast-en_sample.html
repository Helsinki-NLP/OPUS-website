(trg)="24710"> The book is a first-person narrative describing Codreanu 's leadership role in a series of political movements , " The Guard of the National Conscience " , " League of National Christian Defence " , " the Legion of the Archangel Michael " , and finally , the Iron Guard .
(trg)="24711"> His goal within these movements was to defend the newly established Greater Romania against a set of demonised enemies , particularly , the Soviet Union and the Jewish people.[3 ] The narratives are interspersed with quotations from Romanian intellectuals , as well as clippings from contemporary newspapers.[3 ]
