(src)="437848"> Construction was delayed by engineering and financial problems .

(src)="437981"> Havana Vieja was founded by the Spanish in 1519 in the natural harbor of the Bay of Havana .
(src)="437982"> It became a stopping point for the treasure laden Spanish Galleons on the crossing between the New World and the Old World .
(src)="437983"> In the 17th century it was one of the main shipbuilding centers .
(src)="437984"> The city was built in baroque and neoclassic style .
(src)="437985"> Many buildings have fallen in ruin in the later half of the 20th century , but a number are being restored .
(src)="437986"> The narrow streets of Old Havana contain many buildings , accounting for perhaps as many as one-third of the approximately 3,000 buildings found in Old Havana .
(src)="437987"> It is the ancient city formed from the port , the official center and the Plaza de Armas .
(src)="437988"> In 1555 Old Havana was destroyed and burned by the French corsair Jacques de Sores .
(src)="437989"> The pirate had taken Havana easily , plundering the city and burning much of it to the ground .
(src)="437990"> After limiting the scarce defenders , De Sores left without obtaining the enormous wealth that he was hoping to find in Havana .
(src)="437991"> The city remained devastated and set on fire .
(src)="437992"> Since the incident , the Spanish brought soldiers and started building fortresses and walls to protect the city .
(src)="437993"> Castillo de la Real Fuerza was the first fortress built ; initiated in 1558 , the construction was overseen by the engineer Bartolomé Sanchez .
