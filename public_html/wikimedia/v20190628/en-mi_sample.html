(src)="207898"> Ghil'ad Zuckermann ( Hebrew : גלעד צוקרמן‬  , pronounced  [ ɡi'lad ˈtsukeʁman ] , born 1 June 1971 ) is a linguist and revivalist who works in contact linguistics , lexicology and the study of language , culture and identity.[1 ] Zuckermann is professor of Linguistics and Endangered Languages at the University of Adelaide , Australia .
(trg)="98"> Ghil ' ad ( Māori : Kiriata )  Zuckermann ( hiperu : גלעד צוקרמן  , korerotia [ ɡi ' lad ˈtsukeʁman ] , i whanau i te 1 o hune o te tau 1971 ) ko te linguist , me te revivalist nei te mahi i roto i te whakapā linguistics , lexicology me te ako o te reo , te ahurea me te tuakiri .
