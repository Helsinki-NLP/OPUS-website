(trg)="51354"> Hardinge Bridge ( Bengali : হার্ডিঞ্জ ব্রিজ ) is a steel railway bridge over the river Padma located at Paksey in western Bangladesh .
(trg)="51355"> It is named after Lord Hardinge , who was the Viceroy of India from 1910 to 1916 .
(trg)="51356"> The bridge is 1.8 kilometres ( 1.1  mi ) long.[1 ]

(trg)="51402"> There is a 9-kilometre long ( 5.6  mi ) section over open water across Yangcheng Lake in Suzhou.[1 ]

(trg)="51981"> Kuala Lumpur ( /ˈkwɑːlə ˈlʊmpʊər , -pər/ ; Malaysian :  [ ˈkwalə ˈlumpʊr ] ) , officially the Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur ( Malay : Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur ) , or commonly known as KL , is the national capital of Malaysia as well as its largest city .
(trg)="51982"> The only global city in Malaysia , it covers an area of 243  km2 ( 94  sq  mi ) and has an estimated population of 1.73 million as of 2016.[6 ] Greater Kuala Lumpur , also known as the Klang Valley , is an urban agglomeration of 7.25 million people as of 2017.[7 ] It is among the fastest growing metropolitan regions in South-East Asia , in both population and economic development .
