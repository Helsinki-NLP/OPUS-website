(src)="371623"> Mirimi Mathias Kanda ( 2 June 1942 – 30 October 2009)[1 ] was a Zimbabwean track and field athlete born in Enkeldoorn [ 1 ] who competed in the 1964 Summer Olympic men 's marathon,[2 ] finishing 51st and running 2:41.09 in the marathon .
(src)="371624"> He was then selected to run in the 1968 Olympic team for Rhodesia , along with fellow Zimbabwean , Bernard Dzoma , but this team was not allowed to compete in the Olympic Games since Rhodesia did not allow black Africans to vote in national elections .
(src)="371625"> Kanda running experiences during this time are detailed by his Australian coach , John Cheffers , in his book : A Wilderness of Spite.[3 ]
