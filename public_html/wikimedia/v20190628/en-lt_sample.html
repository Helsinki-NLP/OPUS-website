(src)="169555"> The Australian Shepherd is a medium size breed of dog that was developed on ranches in the western United States.[2 ] Despite its name , the breed was not developed in Australia , but rather in the United States where they were seen in the West as early as the 1800s.[3][4][5 ] The breed rose gradually in popularity with the boom of western riding after World War I. They became known to the general public through rodeos , horse shows , and Disney movies made for television .
