(trg)="5378">Muhammad Anwar el-Sadat[1] (25 December 1918 – 6 October 1981) was the third President of Egypt.
(trg)="5379">He served from 15 October 1970 until he was assassinated by Islamic extremists on 6 October 1981.
(src)="4142"> Sadat was born in 1918 into a family with 13 children , and attended a military school . [ 2 ] After his graduation , he was sent to an outpost where he met and became a close friend of Gamal Abdel Nasser .
(src)="4143"> There , the two of them became part of a group of young soldiers who later became the Free Officers group that overthrew the Muhammad Ali Dynasty in the Egyptian Revolution of 1952 . [ 2 ] Nasser became the president after the revolution , and Sadat served as one of Nasser ' s ministers until he died in 1970 .
