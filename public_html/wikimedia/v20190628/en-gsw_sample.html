(src)="76169"> The WCA Board oversees that the applications to hold WCA Competitions meet fair conditions to give all the world similar chances to compete , and finally approves and announces the competitions accordingly .
(src)="76170"> After competitions , they read reports from the WCA delegates about competitions in order to be up to date on the WCA activity worldwide and take part in the discussion of any incidents that occur .
