(src)="147620"> A sand lance or sandlance is a fish belonging to the family Ammodytidae .
(src)="147621"> Several species of sand lances are commonly known as " sand eels " , though they are not related to true eels .
(src)="147622"> Another variant name is launce,[2 ] and all names of the fish are references to its slender body and pointed snout .
(src)="147623"> The family name ( and genus name , Ammodytes ) means " sand burrower " , which describes the sand lance 's habit of burrowing into sand to avoid tidal currents .
