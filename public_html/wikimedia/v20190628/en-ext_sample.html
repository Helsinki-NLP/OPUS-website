(src)="63034"> The Junior Eurovision Song Contest ( French : Concours Eurovision de la Chanson Junior),[1 ] often shortened to JESC , Junior Eurovision or Junior EuroSong , is a song competition which has been organised by the European Broadcasting Union ( EBU ) annually since 2003 and is open exclusively to broadcasters that are members of the EBU.[2 ] It is held in a different European city each year , however the same city can host the contest more than once .
