(src)="301372"> Her speeches at Public events inspired adulation among the women who started sending their girls to schools [ 6 ] .
(src)="301373"> Begum was married to Agha Zaffar Ali Qizilbash , a scion of an aristocratic Afghan family settled in kashmir.[3 ] The couple had three sons , Agha Nasir Ali-IAS , a civil servant who retired as Labour Secretary of India in 1977 , Agha Shaukat Ali , who joined civil services of Pakistan during the partition of India in 1947 .
(src)="301374"> Begum 's youngest son is Agha Ashraf Ali , an academician who retired as Commissioner of Higher Education in Jammu and Kashmir[4 ] The Veiled Suite : The Collected Poem , an anthology written by her grandson Agha Shahid Ali , a noted Kashmiri-American poet , features a poem in memory of her.[5 ] The Government of India awarded her the fourth highest civilian honour of Padma Shri in 1987.[6 ] Later in a Doordarshan interview , she announced to return the award in protest against the undemocratic policies of the Government.[5 ] She moved to the United States in the 1990s and lived there with her son Agha Shaukat Ali until her death in 1999.[7 ]
