(src)="430299"> The gamification of learning is an educational approach to motivate students to learn by using video game design and game elements in learning environments.[1][2 ] The goal is to maximize enjoyment and engagement through capturing the interest of learners and inspiring them to continue learning.[3 ] Gamification , broadly defined , is the process of defining the elements which comprise games that make those games fun and motivate players to continue playing , and using those same elements in a non-game context to influence behaviour.[4 ] In other words gamification is the introduction of game elements in a non-game situation .
