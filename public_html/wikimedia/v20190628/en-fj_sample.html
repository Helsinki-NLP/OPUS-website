(src)="63047"> Artocarpus mariannensis ( Chamorro : dugdug ) , also known as the Marianas breadfruit or the seeded breadfruit , is a species of plant in the mulberry / fig family , Moraceae.[2 ] It is endemic to the Mariana Islands and Guam.[3 ]
