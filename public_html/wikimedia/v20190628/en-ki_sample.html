(src)="125132"> The Indian Ocean is the third largest of the world 's oceanic divisions , covering 70,560,000  km2 ( 27,240,000  sq  mi ) ( approximately 20 % of the water on the Earth 's surface).[1 ] It is bounded by Asia on the north , on the west by Africa , on the east by Australia , and on the south by the Southern Ocean or , depending on definition , by Antarctica.[2 ] It is named after India.[3 ] The Indian Ocean is known as Ratnākara ( Sanskrit : रत्नाकर ) , " the mine of gems " in ancient Sanskrit literature , and as Hind Mahāsāgar ( Hindi : हिन्द महासागर ) , in Hindi .
