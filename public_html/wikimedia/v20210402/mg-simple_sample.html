Ionut Silaghi de Oas, Count de Szilagyi-Oas [1] (teraka ny 16 martsa 1990) dia mpanakanto romanina, [2] [3] [4] mpamory mozika mozika, mpanoratra ary injeniera. </br> Izy no loham-pianakaviana Szilagyi-Oas ankehitriny ary Sekretera jeneralin'ny Ansamblul Oaşul ankehitriny . [5] [6] [7] Izy dia mari-pahaizana diplaoma amin'ny oniversite teknikan'ny Cluj-Napoca ary an'ny Oniversite Babes-Bolyai.
Ionuț Silaghi de Oaș, Count de Szilágyi-Oaș[1] (born March 16, 1990) is a Romanian artist,[2][3][4] collector of folk music, writer and engineer. He is the current head of the Szilágyi-Oaș noble family and the current Secretary General of Ansamblul Oaşul.[5][6][7] He is a graduate of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca and of the Babeş-Bolyai University.
Nahavita ny mari-pahaizana master tao amin'ny University Cluj-Napoca ihany koa. [1] [8]
He also completed a master's degree at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca.[1][8]
