Sharon Osbourne (born 9 October 1952) is a British music manager, television personality, and talent show judge.
Sharon Osbourne (narodená 9. Októbra 1952) je britská hudobná manažérka, televízna osobnosť a porotkyňa talentových šou.
Osbourne first became famous on The Osbournes which was a reality show that showed Osbourne and her family in their daily lives.
Osbourne sa prvýkrát preslávila vďaka reality šou o každodennom živote jej rodiny "The Osbournes".
She has also been a judge on the talent shows The X Factor and America's Got Talent.
Bola tiež porotkyňou v talentových súťažiach The X Factor a America's Got Talent.
Her autobiography, Extreme, which Osbourne wrote about her life, was very successful and has sold 2 million copies.
Jej autobiografia "Extreme", ktorú napísala o svojom živote sa stala veľmi populárnou a predalo sa jej 2 miliónov kópii.
She has recovered from colorectal cancer.
V minuslosti prekonala rakovinu hrubého čreva.
She is married to the rock singer Ozzy Osbourne and she has three children with him named Kelly Osbourne, Jack Osbourne and Aimee Osbourne.
Je vydaná za rokového speváka Ozzy Osbourne s ktorým ma tri deti: Kelly, Jack a Aimme Osbourne.
