Poltika sosyale yew terimo kı mıxtelıf cayanê politika dı gırweniyeno, vêşêr yew estiya hukmati ya zi politiki dı (zey dewleta sosyale u gırwenayışê xızmetanê sosyali) ca gêna, cı rê vaciyena.[1]
Social policy is a term which is applied to various areas of policy, usually within a governmental or political setting (such as the welfare state and study of social services).[1]
No terim şeno rehberi, prensibi, legalizasyon u akivitê kı şertê weşiya merdıman ra piya şınê, inan ra bêro vatış.[2] Merkezê Malcolm Wiener Politika Sosyale Harvard University dı politika sosyale wıni şınasneno "politika pêroyiya u cıewniyayışê weşi, xızmetê merdımi, edaletê suci, mısayış u gırwe dı gırweniyena.[3] Desto bin ra politika sosyale şeno wıni ifade bo, kerdışê kı weşiya mensubanê yew komi rê gırwenenê, mal u xızmeti rê resayışê şari, mal u xızmeti paykerdış, standardê cıwiyayışê merdıman berzkerdış, şertê xırabi miyan ra wedarıtışê[4] Politika sosyal vêşêr çiyê kı standardê merdıman anê cêr, inan wedarına. [5]
It can refer to guidelines, principles, legislation and activities that affect the living conditions conducive to human welfare, such as a person's quality of life. The Department of Social Policy at the London School of Economics defines social policy as "an interdisciplinary and applied subject concerned with the analysis of societies' responses to social need", which seeks to foster in its students a capacity to understand theory and evidence drawn from a wide range of social science disciplines, including economics, sociology, psychology, geography, history, law, philosophy and political science.[2] The Malcolm Wiener Center for Social Policy at Harvard University describes social policy as "public policy and practice in the areas of health care, human services, criminal justice, inequality, education, and labor".[3] Social policy might also be described as actions that affect the well-being of members of a society through shaping the distribution of and access to goods and resources in that society.[4] Social policy often deals with wicked problems.[5]
