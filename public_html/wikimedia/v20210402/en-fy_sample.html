Joaquim Torra i Pla (born 28 December 1962), known as Quim Torra, is a Spanish politician, lawyer, and editor from Catalonia.
Joaquim Torra i Pla (berne op 28 desimber 1962), bekend as Quim Torra, is in Spaanske politikus, abbekaat, and redakteur út Kataloanje.
Torra is a member of the Parliament of Catalonia and is the current President of Catalonia.
Torra is in lid fan it Katalaansje Parlemint en de hjoeddeistige Presidint fan Kataloanje.
Born in 1962 in Blanes, Torra graduated from the Autonomous University of Barcelona before joining the legal profession.
Torra waard berne yn 1962 yn Blanes en studearre ôf op de Autonome Universiteit fan Barselona. Dêrnei gie er de wrâld fan it rjocht yn.
He worked as lawyer and executive for a multinational insurance company for twenty years before starting his own publishing company.
Hy wurke tweintich jier as abbekaat en manager foar in multynasjonaal fersekeringsbedriuw. Dêrnei begûn er syn eigen útjouwingsbedriuw.
He later held senior positions for the City of Barcelona and Generalitat de Catalunya.
Letter krige er wichtige funksjes yn it bestjoer fan Barselona en de Generaliteit fan Kataloanje.
A supporter of Catalan independence, Torra has held senior positions in several pro-independence organisations including the Òmnium Cultural and Assemblea Nacional Catalana.
Hy is in foarstanner fan Katalaanske ûnôfhinklikens. Torra had wichtige funksjes beklaaid yn ferskate pro-ûnôfhinklikensorganisaasjes, lykas it Òmnium Cultural en Assemblea Nacional Catalana.
He was elected to the Parliament of Catalonia at the 2017 regional election as an independent candidate for the pro-independence Junts per Catalunya electoral alliance.
Hy waard keazen ta it Katalaanske Parlemint by de regionale ferkiezingen fan as kandidaat foar Junts per Catalunya.
In May 2018 he was elected 131st President of Catalonia[1] after the Spanish courts blocked three other candidates.
Yn maaie 2018 waard Torra ferkeazen as 131e Presidint fan Kataloanje[1], nei't Spaanske hôven trije oare kandidaten blokkearre hienen.
Torra is married to teacher Carola Miró.[4] They have three children, two girls and a boy.[4]
Torra is troud mei learares Carola Miró.[4] Sy ha trije bern, twa dochters en in soan.[4]

Aphantasia is a mental condition characterized by an inability to voluntarily visualize mental imagery.[1] Many people with aphantasia also report an inability to recall sounds, smells, or sensations of touch.
Afantasy is in mentale oandwaning wêr't ien net yn steat is om mentale bylden frijwillich te fisualisearjen. In protte minsken mei afantasia ûnderfine ek in ûnfermogen om sawol lûden, roken, as oanreitsjen te kinne ynbylden.
Some also report prosopagnosia, the inability to recognize faces.[2]
Der binne ek minsken dy't ek prosopagnosia ûnderfine, it ûnfermogen om gesichten te heuge.
The phenomenon was first described by Francis Galton in 1880[1] but has since remained relatively unstudied.
It fenomeen waard foar it earst beskreaun yn 1880 troch Francis Galton mar is sûnt noch net folle bestudearre.
Interest in the phenomenon renewed after the publication of a study in 2015 conducted by a team led by Professor Adam Zeman of the University of Exeter,[2] which also coined the term aphantasia.[3] Research on the condition is still scarce.[6][7]
