Thomas Daniel "Tim" Conway (desembre nascut 15, 1933 en Willoughby, Ohio) és un actor estatunidenc guanyador d'un premi Emmy.
Thomas Daniel "Tim" Conway (born December 15, 1933 in Willoughby, Ohio) is an Emmy Award-winning American comedian and actor.

Ha treballat en sitcoms, esquetxos de comèdia, i en pel·lícules.
He has worked in sitcoms, comedy sketches, and in movies.
Conway és conegut per la sitcom McHale's Navy de 1960 sobre la Segona Guerra Mundial com a Charles Parker.
Conway is best known for the 1960s World War II sitcom McHale's Navy as Ensign Charles Parker.
Pels espectadors més joves, Conway és conegut com Barnacle Boy en la sitcom animada Spongebob Squarepants.
To younger viewers, Conway is known for Barnacle Boy in the animated sitcom Spongebob Squarepants.
