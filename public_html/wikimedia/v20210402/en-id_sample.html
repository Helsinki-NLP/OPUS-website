Cultural legacy
Ivan Stepanovych Mazepa (Ukrainian: Іван Степанович Мазепа, Russian: Ива́н Степа́нович Мазе́па, Polish: Jan Mazepa Kołodyński; March 30 [O.S. March 20] 1639 – October 2 [O.S. September 21] 1709)[1] was the Cossack Hetman of the Hetmanate in Left-bank Ukraine, from 1687–1708, and Prince of the Holy Roman Empire ad personam 1707–1709.[2] He was famous as a patron of the arts, and also played an important role in the Battle of Poltava where after learning of Peter I's intent to relieve him as acting Hetman of Ukraine and replace him with Alexander Menshikov, he deserted his army and sided with Charles of Sweden.
Ivan Stepanovych Mazepa (Ukrainian: Іван Степанович Мазепа, Russian: Ива́н Степа́нович Мазе́па, Polish: Jan Mazepa Kołodyński; March 30 [O.S. March 20] 1639 – October 2 [O.S. September 21] 1709),[1] was the Cossack Hetman of the Hetmanate in Left-bank Ukraine, from 1687–1708, and Prince of the Holy Roman Empire ad personam 1707-1709.[2] He was famous as a patron of the arts, and also played an important role in the Battle of Poltava where after learning of Peter I's intent to relieve him as acting Hetman of Ukraine and replace him with Alexander Menshikov, he deserted his army and sided with Charles of Sweden.
Mazepa was probably born on March 30, 1639,[1] in Mazepyntsi, near Bila Tserkva, then part of the Kiev Voivodeship in the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth (today – Drozdy rural council, Bila Tserkva Raion), into a noble Ruthenian-Lithuanian family. His mother was Maryna Mokievska (1624–1707) (known from 1674–75 by her monastic name Maria Magdalena),[4] and his father was Stefan Adam Mazepa (?-1666). Maryna Mokievska came from the family of a Cossack officer who fought alongside Bohdan Khmelnytsky.

Sutan Amrull (/ˈsuːtɒn ˈɒmruːl/; born June 14, 1974), also known as Raja and Raja Gemini, is an American make-up artist and drag performer.
Sutan Amrull (lahir 14 Juni 1974), juga dikenal sebagai Raja dan Raja Gemini, adalah seorang penata rias dan drag queen asal Amerika Serikat.
He is best known for his work on the reality television show America's Next Top Model,[1] serving as the show's make-up artist for nine cycles (fourth through twelve), and as the winner of Season 3 of RuPaul's Drag Race.
Ia lebih dikenal untuk karyanya di acara realitas televisi America's Next Top Model,[1] dimansa ia bekerja sebagai penata rias untuk sembilan musim (keempat sampai keduabelas), dan sebagai pemenang Musim 3 dari RuPaul s Drag Race.
Amrull's clients include Dita von Teese, Pamela Anderson, Paulina Porizkova, Iman, Tyra Banks, Iggy Azalea, and Twiggy.[2] Since 2009, Amrull has been make-up artist to singer Adam Lambert for print media, live U.S. appearances, and Lambert's international 2010 Glam Nation Tour.
Ia bekerja sebagai penata rias untuk beberapa artis seperti Dita von Teese, Pamela Anderson, Paulina Porizkova, Iman, Tyra Banks, Iggy Azalea, dan Twiggy.[2] Sejak tahun 2009, Amrull telah menjadi penata rias untuk penyanyi Adam Lambert untuk media cetak, penampilan live AS, dan tur internasional Lambert pada tahun 2010 yang bertajuk Glam Nation Tour.
Amrull was born in Baldwin Park, California.
Amrull lahir di Baldwin Park, California.
At the age of about six his family moved to Indonesia (where his father is from) and stayed there for six years before returning to the U.S.[3]
Pada usia sekitar 6 tahun keluarganya pindah ke Indonesia (di mana ayahnya berasal) dan tinggal di sana selama enam tahun sebelum kembali ke AS.[3]
When he was sixteen, Amrull got into the drag scene by attending L.A. nightclubs with his friends.
Ketika ia berusia enam belas tahun, Amrull masuk ke dunia drag dengan menghadiri klub-klub malam di LOs Angeles dengan teman-temannya.
He was part of a more rebellious sector of drag that was inspired by the goth and punk subcultures.[4]
Dia adalah bagian dari sektor drag yang lebih memberontak yang terinspirasi oleh subkultur goth dan punk.[4]
He attended university as an art student for 2 years before realising it wasn't for him.
Ia berkuliah sebagai mahasiswa seni selama 2 tahun sebelum menyadari bahwa itu tidak untuk dia.
After that he decided to follow his own artistic path to pursue a career in make up artistry and female impersonation.[5]
Setelah itu dia memutuskan untuk mengikuti jalan artisnya sendiri untuk mengejar karir di ketatariasan dan peniruan perempuan.[5]
Raja was the first Asian American winner of the Drag Race series.[11]
Raja adalah orang Asia Amerika pertama yang memenangkan seri Drag Race.[11]
In 2014 Raja joined the Drag Race Battle of the Seasons tour where she performed a lipsync duet with Manila Luzon to the Babymetal song "Gimme Chocolate!!".
Di tahun 2014, Raja bergabung dengan tur Drag Race Battle of the Seasons di mana dia melakukan duet lipsync dengan Manila Luzon ke dalam lagu Babymetal "Gimme Chocolate!!".
Since 2014 he has presented the weekly WOWPresents YouTube series RuPaul's Drag Race Fashion Photo RuView with fellow Drag Race alumna Raven.[16]
Sejak 2014, ia telah membawakan serial YouTube mingguan WOWPresents RuPaul s Drag Race Fashion Photo RuView dengan sesama alumnus Drag Race Raven.[16]
In February 2017 he starred as an intergalactic creature in the music video for Blondie's single "Fun".[17]
Pada bulan Februari 2017 ia membintangi sebagai makhluk luar angkasa dalam video musik Blondie "Fun".[17]

He was a contestant on the third season of RuPaul's Drag Race, performing as his drag persona Raja Gemini.[6] He was a member in the "Heathers" clique on the program,[7][8] which took its name from the popular cult film, Heathers.
Ia adalah seorang kontestan pada musim ketiga RuPaul s Drag Race, tampil sebagai drag queen Raja Gemini.[6] Dia adalah anggota dalam clique "Heathers" pada program,[7][8] yang mengambil nama dari film kultus, Heathers.
In the final episode, after a lip sync battle with Manila Luzon, Raja was declared the winner of the show's third season and crowned America's Next Drag Superstar.[9] As part of his duties as the third season winner of Drag Race, Amrull toured the U.S. and Canada on Logo's Drag Race Tour in 2011.[10]
Di akhir episode, setelah lip sync battle dengan Manila Luzon, Raja dinyatakan sebagai pemenang dari musim ketiga acara ini dan dinobatkan sebagai America's Next Drag Superstar.[9] Sebagai bagian dari tugasnya sebagai pemenang musim ketiga Drag Race, Amrull melakukan tur ke AS dan Kanada di Logo's Drag Race Tour pada tahun 2011.[10]
In May 2011, Amrull released his first single as Raja, titled "Diamond Crowned Queen".[12] The single debuted at number fifty on the Billboard Hot Dance Club Songs,[13] before peaking at number thirty-five.[14] On August 21, 2012, his second single "Sublime" was released.
Pada Mei 2011, Amrull merilis single pertamanya sebagai Raja, yang berjudul "Diamond Crowned Queen".[12] Singel tersebut debut di nomor 50 di Billboard Hot Dance Club Songs,[13] sebelum memuncak di nomor 35.[14] Pada tanggal 21 Agustus 2012, single kedua "Sublime" dirilis.
His third single, "Zubi Zubi Zubi" (roughly translating to "Dance Dance Dance"), was released June 1, 2013.[15]
Single ketiganya, "Zubi Zubi Zubi", dirilis tanggal 1 Juni 2013.[15]
