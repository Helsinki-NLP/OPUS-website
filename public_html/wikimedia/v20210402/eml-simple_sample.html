Un numàr päri l'é un numàr intrég c'al pol isor divis par du sénza averog dal rest, quindi un numàr intrég che quanda al vena divis par du al résta un numàr intrég.
An even number is an integer that can be divided by two and remain an integer or has no remainder.

Un numàr sa n'le miga päri al s'disa dispari, e quanda un numàr dispari l'é divis par du, a risulta un numàr ca n'le miga pu intreg
An integer that is not an even number is an odd number. If an even number is divided by two, the result is another integer. On the other hand, an odd number, when divided by two, will result in a non-integer.
I polon isòr päri anca i numàr negativ.
Since even numbers are integers, negative numbers can be even.
