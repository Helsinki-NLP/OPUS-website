Ionut Silaghi de Oas, Ipha de Szilágyi-Oaş [1] (natawo Marso 16, 1990) mao ang usa ka sa Romania mag-aawit, songwriter, [2] [3] [4] [5] [6] producer, [7] maniningil sa folk musika, tagsulat, aristocrat, negosyante [8] ug engineer. [9] Siya ang karon nga pinuno sa Szilágyi-Oaș nga halangdon nga pamilya ug ang karon Secretary General sa Ansamblul Oaşul . [10] [11] [12] Siya usa ka graduate sa Technical University sa Cluj-Napoca ug sa Babeş-Bolyai University.
Ionuț Silaghi de Oaș, Count de Szilágyi-Oaș[1] (born March 16, 1990) is a Romanian singer, songwriter,[2][3][4][5][6] producer,[7] collector of folk music, author, aristocrat, entrepreneur[8] and engineer.[9] He is the current head of the Szilágyi-Oaș noble family and the current Secretary General of Ansamblul Oaşul.[10][11][12] He is a graduate of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca and of the Babeş-Bolyai University.

He also completed a master's degree at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca.[1][13]
Ionut Silaghi de Oas sakop usab sa Broadcast Music, Inc. [1] ug sa SoundExchange [2] .
Silaghi de Oaș is also a member of Broadcast Music, Inc.[14] and of SoundExchange [15].
