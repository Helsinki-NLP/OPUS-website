Petre Mădălin Constantinescu (n. 10 august 1996, Curtea de Argeș, România), cunoscut sub numele de scenă 7EGAS, este un producător de muzică electronică.
Constantinescu Petre Mădălin (Romanian was born on 10 August 1996 in Curtea De Arges, România), better known by his stage name 7EGAS, is a romanian musician, DJ, remixer and music producer.
În anul 2012, 7EGAS și-a început cariera sa muzicala alături de fostul său manager, Călin Sîrbu, în orasul său natal.
In 2012, 7EGAS began his musical career with his former manager, Călin Sirbu, in his hometown. The artist first started with the Vegas scene name, put even set by the former manager, but soon the artist thought that a minor change of name would be better, rebranding his stage name in 7EGAS. In 2015, he made the decision to give up his manager.
Artistul a început initial cu numele de scenă Vegas, pusă chiar de fostul manager, dar la scurt timp artistul s-a gândit că ar fi mai bine o schimbare minoră de nume, rebrenduindu-și numele de scenă în 7EGAS.
In 2016, the artist signed a contract with Sony Music Entertainment for his songs: "You Do not Know Me," "Set Me Free," "You Trust Me". A year after signing the contract, he signed up with Sony Music Entertainment, but the songs remain under Sony Music. The artist's most famous piece is You Do not Know Me, with over 9,000 units sold on iTunes.

Ted Bundy was born in 1946 in Burlington, Vermont, USA.[1] He lived with his mother Louise Cowell and grandfather Sam Cowell when he was a child.[1] Bundy and his mother moved to Washington where she married a man named John Culpepper Bundy.[1] As a child in Washington, Bundy was a good student and went to church often, yet he found it hard to make friends and he began stealing.
[1] Ca copil în Washington, Bundy era un student bun și mergea frecvent la biserică, totuși îi era greu să-și facă prieteni și a început să fure .
As a young man Bundy became involved in politics and proposed to a woman that he had dated in high school.
Încă de tânăr, Bundy s-a implicat în politică și a cerut-o de soție pe iubita acestuia din liceu .
