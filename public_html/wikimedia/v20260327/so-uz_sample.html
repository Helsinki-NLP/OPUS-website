Linebet waa shirkad sharad caalami ah oo shati ku leh Curacao. Waxaa la sameeyay 2019 waxayna ka diiwaan gashan tahay Qubrus.
Linebet - bu Curasao eGaming litsenziyasi ostida litsenziyalangan xalqaro tikish kompaniyasi.
Mulkiilaha buug-sameeyaha waa Talkeetna Ltd.
U 2019 yilda tashkil etilgan va Kiprda ro'yxatdan o'tgan.
Ogolaanshaha ugu weyn ee fulinta hawlaha berrinka ciyaaraha sharadka ee internetka iyo bixinta noocyada gargaarka ee berrinkii sharadka waa liisan hoosaadka No. 8048/JAZ2016-053. Shatiga waxaa bixiyay Guddiga sharadka Curacao.
Tikish sohasida yordam turlarini taklif qiluvchi Internet orqali sport tikish sohasidagi faoliyatni amalga oshirish uchun asosiy ruxsat 8048/JAZ2016-053 sub-litsenziya hisoblanadi.
Ogolaanshaha ugu weyn ayaa la soo saaray iyada oo ay ugu wacan tahay ururka xeebta Antlephone N.V., kaas oo si la mid ah u wareejiyay oggolaanshaha hoosaadka ee ururka Qubrus Talkeetna Ltd.
Litsenziya Kyurasao tikish komissiyasi tomonidan berilgan.
Ogolaanshahani wuxuu u oggolaanayaa shirkadda sharadka inay ku shaqeyso adduunka oo dhan iyadoo leh xaddidaadyo gaar ah. Shirkadda ayaa ka mamnuuc ah Maraykanka, UK, Ruushka, Spain, Italy iyo Netherlands.
Asosiy ruxsatnoma dengiz bo'yidagi Antillephone N.V. tashkiloti uchun berilgan, u xuddi shu tarzda Kiprning Talkeetna Ltd tashkilotiga tegishli ruxsatnomani topshirgan.
