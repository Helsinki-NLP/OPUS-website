Avram Noam Chomsky (lair 7 Désèmber 1928) atawa No'am Homski entu atu ahli basa deri Amrik, pilsup, tukang getol pulitik, mualip, ama dosèn.
Avram Noam Chomsky (born December 7, 1928) is an American linguist, philosopher, political activist, author, and lecturer.

Dia entu Propèsor Institut ama pangsiunan propèsor di Institut Tèhnolohi Masacusèt.
He is an Institute Professor and professor emeritus of linguistics at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
Menara Élisabèt, bekèn sebagé Jam Inggris (Big Ben)
The Elizabeth Tower, known as Big Ben
Jem Inggris (hurup Arab Gundul: جم إڠݢريس; Inggris: Big Ben) entu nama poyokan bakal kelenèngan nyang gondal-gandul di menara jem nyang ada di blèlirnya Ruma Mejelis Bicara, Londen, Inggris.[1] Resminya mah, ni menara digero'nya Menara Élisabèt.
Big Ben is the nickname of a bell that hangs in the clock tower at the northern end of the Houses of Parliament in Westminster, London, England.[1] Officially, the tower itself is called Elizabeth Tower.
Tadénya, ni menara cuman dibilang Menara Jem, cuman dibalik nama dah tu pas bulan Sèptèmber 2012 buat ngerayain Gocap Tahon Intennya Ratu Élisabèt II.[2] Cumanan mah, orang, masup juga' orang nyang pada ngambrek di Londen, pada nggero'nya Big Ben anu tumannya begitu, kon, bahnanya èmang segedé alaihim.
It was previously known as just the Clock Tower, but was renamed in September 2012 as a tribute to the Diamond Jubilee of Queen Elizabeth II.[2] However, most people, including those that live in London, call the tower "Big Ben" because it is very large.
Ni menara dirancang ama Edmund Beckett Denison ama makan wayah ampé 13 tahon punya lama cuman bakal ngebangun ni menara nyang marinya rampung selamanya tahon 1859.[1] Tu jem pepes nyala dah semparakan entu tahon, kecuali berapa bulanan doang pas tahon 1976 nyang tu jem ngadat ampé kudu didandanin dulu.
Designed by Edmund Beckett Denison, the clock took 13 years to build and it was completed in 1859.[3] It has worked continuously since then except for a few months in 1976 when it broke down and had to be fixed.

Sigmun Peroid atawa Sigmun Proid (hurup Arab Gundul: سيݢموند فرويد; Dèt: Sigmund Freud; Morawi, 6 Méi1856 – Londen, 23 Sèptèmber 1939) entu atu dukun sarap (neurologist) deri Ostenrèk.[1] Dia nyang nemuin pengobatan sakit sèipi roda (mental illness) ama sakit sarap (neurosis) paké wasilah terindil jiwa (psychoanalysis).[2]
Sigmund Freud (Moravia, 6 May 1856 – London, 23 September 1939) was an Austrian neurologist (a person who treats the nervous system).[2] He invented the treatment of mental illness and neurosis by means of psychoanalysis.[3]

Early life
Scarlett Johansson berojol di Niu-Yorek Kota, Niu-Yorek 22 Nopèmber 1984.
Scarlett Johansson was born in New York City, New York on November 22, 1984.
Dia punya baba, nama Karsten Johansson, entu tukang rancang gedong berojolan Dènemarken deri Kopenhagen,[1] dia punya engkong deri baba, nama Ejner Johansson, entu tukang nulis lakon ama dalang pèlem.
Her father, Karsten Johansson, is a Danish-born architect originally from Copenhagen,[2] and her paternal grandfather, Ejner Johansson, was a screenwriter and director.
Dia punya enya', nama Melanie Sloan, entu juragan pèlem, entu deri kulawarga Yahudi Askénasi deri Berong.[2][3][4] Si Melanie punya keruhun mijah ke Niu-Yorek deri Mingsek, Ruslan jaman kijir.[5] Dia juga' ngelah empo', nama Vanessa, nyang juga' jadi tukang lakon; abang, nama Adrian; abang kembarang, nama Hunter (nyang tau nongtot ama dia di pèlem Manny & Lo);[6] ama abang sebaba, nama Christian, deri babanya punya kawin nyang pertama.[7]
Her mother, Melanie Sloan, a producer, comes from an Ashkenazi Jewish family from the Bronx.[3][4][5] Melanie's ancestors emigrated to New York from Minsk, Tsarist Russia.[6] She has an older sister, Vanessa, also an actress; an older brother, Adrian; a twin brother, Hunter (who appeared with her in the film Manny & Lo);[7] and an older half-brother, Christian, from her father's first marriage.[8]
