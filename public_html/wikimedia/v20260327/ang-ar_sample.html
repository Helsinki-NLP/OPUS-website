Timitar festival is a moroccan musical festival organized every summer in Agadir .
مهرجان تيميتار هو مهرجان سنوي للموسيقى بمدينة أكادير في المغرب.
The begining The timitar festival was launched by aziz akhnouch " the president of the regional council of souss massa daraa " , and the wilaya of the city of agadir , that first edition was in 2004 . The name of the festival was chosen by the politic hassan ourid which means " signs " in tachlhit the aim of the festival was to bring together the amazigh artists and world wide music . timitar by the years 2006 This edition was opened by jimmy clev and closed by cheb mami , many artists attended this edition some of them from cuba , colombia and even iran , the others were chaabi style artists just like najat aatabou , mohammed maghni . A training workshop in electronic music and hiphop was also organized for young moroccan talents in cooperation with the french institute in the city.
