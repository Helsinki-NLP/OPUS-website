Mapaku (//ˈfɛtkI-ʊk//, [ˈfɛtkuk]) ke hlama ya bogobe bjo bo gadikilwego bja setšo bja Afrika Borwa.
Igwinya (//ˈfɛtkI-ʊk//, [ˈfɛtkuk]) liyinhlama yesinkwa esithosiwe ngokwesiko laseNingizimu Afrika.

Le paku ke karolo ya setšo sa Afrika Borwa.
Igwinya liyingxenye yesiko laseNingizimu Afrika.
Go swana le kuku ya Johnny go tšwa Caribbean, Oliebol go tšwa Netherlands, le sopaipillas go tšwa Mexico.
Lifana nekhekhe likaJohnny laseCaribbean, I-oliebol yaseDashi, kanye ne-sopaipillas yaseMexico.
