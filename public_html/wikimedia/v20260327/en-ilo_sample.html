Eufranio "Franny" C. Eriguel is a Filipino physician and politician from the Eriguel political clan of La Union[1][2][3], who has served three terms as Mayor of Agoo, La Union and three terms as Congressman for the Second District of La Union.[4] His wife Sandra Y. Eriguel[1] currently serves as Congressman for the Second District of La Union, while his daughter Stefanie Ann C. Eriguel currently serves as Mayor of Agoo.[5]
Ni Eufranio "Franny" Chan Eriguel, M.D. ket duktor ken pulitiko iti dinastiya nga Eriguel ti La Union[1][2][3]. Baket na ni Sandra Y. Eriguel nga Kongresista ti Ikadua nga Distrito ti La Unon, ken anacna ni Stefanie Ann C. Eriguel nga Alcalde ti Agoo, La Union.[5]

Eriguel came to national attention in 2016 when he survived an alleged assassination attempt[8] during the campaign period for the 2016 General Election[8], and when he was included by Philippine President Rodrigo Duterte as one of the local government officials and legislators allegedly involved in illegal drug trade[9]in his August 16 "I am sorry for my country" speech[10][11] - an allegation which he and other La Union politicians have denied.[12]

Ti Pansino ket maysa kadagiti nagun-od a content-creator iti YouTube, ken naipalista iti pagilian a Forbes' Top Influencers: Food list iti 2017.
Pansino has hosted the internet series Nerdy Nummies since 2011, which won her a Shorty Award and earned her five Streamy Award nominations.
Nangato ti Pansino iti intednet series a Nerdy Nummies babaen iti 2011, nga agngayyaman kaniada ti Shorty Award ken agserrek ti lima a Streamy Award nga nominasyon.

She released her debut single "Perfect Together" in 2015; she is a classically trained singer.[8]
Iniluad na ti debu a single na a "Perfect Together" iti 2015; inus-uset a mang-tren a mangantay a mannurat.
Rosanna Pansino was born in Seattle, King County, Washington, where she was raised alongside her sister Molly Lu.[9] She is of Italian, Croatian, German, and Irish ancestry.[10] Throughout her childhood, she was known by friends and family as a "nerdy" and "awkward" kid, and in school, she struggled with dyslexia.[11][12] She originally wanted to become an actress,[13] and after attending Pacific Lutheran University, she moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting.[11] At one stage, Pansino was a teacher.[14]
Dagiti kaliayo gapu iti Italyano, Croatian, Aleman, ken Irish nga pannakabuyan. Iti tattao nga gapu iti childhood na, agkakastoy daytoy iti ka-amigos ken pamilya nga "nerdy" ken "awkward" a taga-taltalasang, ken iti eskwela, agkakasta daytoy iti dyslexia. Sika ket agtuturay a maging artista, ken kas iti pannakasursurat iti Pacific Lutheran University, isu nga nagbalin nga magtuturay iti Los Angeles tapno ipusay ti pag-arte.

Pansino began her YouTube channel in 2009 after she was encouraged by a few of her friends who had also worked on YouTube, and had started making videos to get more comfortable in front of a camera.[1] After her early baking videos gained a steady following, viewers eventually started to request more.[19] Because she had never seen a baking show on television or the Internet, Pansino decided to create the Nerdy Nummies series.
Nagsimula ni Pansino ti kanayon a YouTube idi 2009 pagpateg ken pinaagsuruandaan ti maysa a pundo ti sadiay kakabsatna a maysa a nagtrabaho met iti YouTube, ken nagsimulag daytoy a mang-ugma ti video tapno maidaduadu iti kameran. Tapno itultuloy ti panagserrek dagiti uneg a baking video, nagsumrek met ti panagkumpirma ti viewers ken pagpapangala ti maduma a kasapulan. Tunggalinanna, no adda nadadael a pannakitaan ti baking show iti telebisyon wenno Internet, nagpasiar ti Pansino a mangipakalap iti seriong Nerdy Nummies.
She later revealed that in her early years of YouTube while she had still worked as an actress that she had to choose between acting or YouTube, the ultimatum given by her agent.[13] She remained adamant that she would not quit YouTube, despite the risks being taken at the time.[20] Pansino told Cosmopolitan in 2015: " At this point, I wasn't making any money on YouTube.
