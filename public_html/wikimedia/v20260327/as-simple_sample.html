সনামহী ধর্ম (Sanamahism) (Meitei: ꯁꯅꯥꯃꯍꯤ ꯂꯥꯏꯅꯤꯡ), বা মীতৈ লাইনীঙ বা মনিপুরী ধর্ম (Manipuri religion) বা কঙলৈ ধর্ম (Kanglei religion) হৈছে ধর্মর উপাসনা করা এক প্রাচীন পুর্বপুরুষ।।[1][2] [3][4]
Sanamahism (Meitei: ꯁꯅꯥꯃꯍꯤ ꯂꯥꯏꯅꯤꯡ), also known as Manipuri religion or Kanglei religion, is an ancient ancestor worshipping religion. It is the indigenous religion of the Meitei people and the tribal people of Manipur.[2][3] It is a polytheistic religion. In Sanamahism, thousands of Gods are worshipped in several pantheons.[4][5]

Shamim Hossen (বাংলা: শামীম হোসেন; Born: 10 August 1992) is a Bangladeshi journalist, songwriter, singer, actor and theater director. He has written and directed more than 30 songs as well as more than 100 plays and short films. He works in the sports department of Bangladesh's most popular first-class newspaper Daily Jugantor.
