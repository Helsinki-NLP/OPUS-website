 <s id="46">Say ingles a managsulat Christopher Smart (1722–1771) sikatoy nipriso ed mental asylum manlupud Mayo 1757 anggad Enero 1763.</s>
 <s id="47">Si Smart so inawat ed St. Luke's Hospital for Lunatics, Upper Moorfields, London, nen 6 Mayo 1757.</s>
 <s id="48">Sikatoy inawit diman na katulangan ton si John Newbery, anggaman nayarin sikatoy nipriso ed sakey a pribadon ospital parad saray buang.</s>

 <s id="50">Anggaman dakel ed saray kapanaonan to so mipakna a si Smart et "sintinsiaan", nanduruma iray salaysay nipaakar ed kipapasen to tan saray nansumpalan na satan, tan walaray maniisip a sikatoy agmatunong.</s>
 <s id="51">Si Smart et "agnasarag a paabigen" legan a walad St. Luke, tan sanen naupot so pundo parad pangasikaso ed sikato et inyalis ed Mr. Say asylum nen Potter, Bethnal Green.</s>
