Mini World is a sandbox game operated by Shenzhen Miniwan Technology Co., Ltd. As of May 2025, it has accumulated over 1 billion registered users worldwide, with more than 100 million monthly active players, over 60% of whom are teenagers. In the game, players take on the role of Milastars, gathering various resources through exploration and crafting tools to build their own creations.
《迷你世界》是一款由深圳市迷你玩科技有限公司运营的沙盒游戏。截至2025年5月，全球累计注册人数超过10亿，其每月活躍玩家人数已超过一亿，其中超過六成为青少年。[1]游戏中玩家将扮演迷拉星人，通过探索获得各种资源并制作道具，打造属于自己的建筑。

Development History
发展历程
On December 26, 2015, Mini World was launched on Android, iOS, and PC platforms in mainland China.[2] On December 8, 2017, Tencent partnered with Shenzhen Miniwan Technology Co., Ltd. to launch a Mini World page on WeGame. On December 14, Mini World was officially released on WeGame. That same year, the game surpassed 24 million monthly active users, ranking third among mobile applications in mainland China.
