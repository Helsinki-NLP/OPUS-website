Attah Issah bee Atta Issah nyɛla Ghana nima siyaasa nira ŋun be lɛm paati (National Democratic Congress) ka nyɛ "Accountant" zaŋ n-ti paati ŋɔ tuma duzuɣu din be Ankara.
Attah Issah or Atta Issah be one of de politicians in Ghana wey dey (National Democratic Congress) he be "Accountant" for en party headquarters wey dey Accra.

Ŋun nyɛ lɛm paati jintɔra zaŋ n-ti Sagnarigu piibu- piibu yaɣili din be Northern Region n-ti yuuni 2024 Ghanaian general election.[1][2]
Na he chop de parliamentary election for en constituency (Sagnarigu) in de Northern Region in de 2024 Ghanaian general election.[1][2]
Yuuni 2023 lɛm paati (National Democratic Congress) mabiligu piibu- piibu, Attah Issah daa nya la nasara ni kalinli din yiɣisi 801 ka chɛ ŋun daa miri bɛ kpaɣiri ŋɔ ni Alhassan Bashir Fuseini din daa niŋ ka o nya kalinli din yiɣisi 333.[3][4][5][6][7]
For di 2023 NDC (National Democratic Congress), primaries elections wey happen, Attah Issah win am wit 801 votes, e pass Alhassan Bashir Fuseini wey get 333 votes.[1][2][3][4][5]
