Ne Zha 2 is a 2025 Chinese computer-animated movie produced by Beijing Enlight Media. It was directed by Jiao Zi. It is adapted from the novel Fengshen Yanyi and the sequel to Ne Zha (2019).

Ne Zha 2 is about a young boy being thought as devil (even though he was kind and friendly), everyone were disgusted but afraid, all except his one and only friend Ao Bing, together they went through many challenges. But together they conquered the evil and built a better world!
