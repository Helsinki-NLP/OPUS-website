Balochistan (Urdu: صوبہ بلوچستان) is a province in Pakistan.
Balochistan yanki ne a ƙasar Pakistan.

The capital of Balochistan is Quetta.
Quetta ne babban birnin yankin.
Balochistan has a population of about 10 million people and an area of 134,051 mi2 or (347,190 km2). It covers 45% of Pakistani territory; in terms of area the province of Balochistan is greater in area than Republic of the Congo but smaller than Germany.

Baluchistan (Chief commissioners province) - seceded from British India and became part of Pakistan in 1947.
Baluchistan (Babban kwamishinonin lardin) - ya ɓalle daga Indiya ta Birtaniya ya zama wani yanki na Pakistan a 1947.
On the 30th of June in 1947 the Khan of Kalat joined Pakistan; tribal gathering and municipality of Quetta also declared for Pakistan.
A ranar 30 ga watan Yuni a shekarar 1947 Khan na Kalat ya shiga Pakistan; An kuma ayyana taron kabilanci da gundumar Quetta ga Pakistan.
Baluchistan States Union - The BSU was formed after the formal Accession of four individual princely states into the new Dominion of Pakistan on the 31st of March in 1948, apart from the enclave of Gwadar that was in cessation from Oman to Pakistan on the 8th of September in 1958.
Kungiyar Jihohin Baluchistan - BSU an kafa ta ne bayan shigar da wasu jahohi hudu masu sarauta a kan sabon Mulkin Pakistan a ranar 31 ga Maris na 1948, baya ga yankin Gwadar wanda ya daina daga Oman zuwa Pakistan a ranar 8 ga Satumba. a 1958.
Both CCP & BSU became the New Province of Balochistan on the 1st of July in 1970, after the dissolution of the former West Pakistan and is an “Integral Part of Pakistan”.
Dukansu CCP & BSU sun zama Sabon Lardin Balochistan a ranar 1 ga Yuli a 1970, bayan rugujewar tsohuwar Pakistan ta Yamma kuma tana "Haɗin kai na Pakistan".￼
In common with the other provinces of Pakistan, Balochistan has a parliamentary system of government.
Daidai da sauran lardunan Pakistan, Balochistan yana da tsarin gwamnati na majalisar dokoki.
The ceremonial head of the province is the Governor, who is appointed by the President of Pakistan on the advice of the provincial Chief Minister.
Shugaban bikin na lardin shine Gwamna, wanda shugaban Pakistan ya naɗa bisa shawarar babban ministan lardin.
The chief executive of the province is the Chief Minister who is normally the leader of the largest party or alliance in the provincial assembly.
Babban jami'in zartarwa na lardin shi ne babban minista wanda galibi shi ne shugaban babbar jam'iyya ko kawance a majalisar lardin.
The unicameral Provincial Assembly of Balochistan comprises 65 seats of which 4% are reserved for non-Muslims and 16% for women only.
Majalisar Lardin Balochistan ta unicameral ta ƙunshi kujeru 65 wanda kashi 4% aka ware wa waɗanda ba Musulmi ba, kashi 16% na mata ne kawai.
The judicial branch of government is carried out by the Balochistan High Court, based in Quetta, and headed by a Chief Justice.
Babban kotun Balochistan ne ke gudanar da sashen shari'a a Quetta, kuma wani babban alkalin kotun ne ke jagoranta.

Dagestan is a federal subject of the Russian Federation in the Caucasus region.
Dagestan yanki ne na Tarayyar Rasha a yankin Caucasus.
The name comes from two Persian words, dag which means mountain, and stan which means land.
Sunan ya fito ne daga kalmomin Farisa guda biyu, dag wanda ke nufin tsaunuka, da stan wanda ke nufin ƙasa.
Dagestan has a population of nearly 3 million, and its people are known as Dagestani.
Dagestan tana da yawan jama'a kusan miliyan 3, kuma ana kiran mutanenta da sunan Dagestan.
Its capital city is Makhachkala.
Babban birninta shine Makhachkala.

Dagestan has the status of "republic" in Russia and has its own constitution and official languages.
Dagestan yana da matsayi na "jamhuriya" a Rasha kuma yana da nasa tsarin mulki da harshen sa na hukuma.
Republics in Russia are the home of its ethnic minorities (those who are not Russians), and usually one or two nationalities have a majority of the population in each republic.
Jamhuriya a Rasha gida ne na ƙananan ƙabilu (waɗanda ba Rashawa ba), kuma yawanci kasa daya ko biyu ne ke da mafi yawan al'ummar kowace jamhuriya.
But in Dagestan, there are over 30 different nationalities (the largest of which are the Avar, Lak, Dargwa, Lezgian, and Tabasaran), and none of these form a majority.
Amma a Dagestan, akwai fiye da 30 na ƙasashe daban-daban (mafi girma daga cikinsu shine Avar, Lak, Dargwa, Lezgian, da Tasaran), kuma babu ɗayan waɗannan da ke da rinjaye.
This is because the mountains in the region make it hard for people to move around.
Hakan ya faru ne saboda tsaunukan da ke yankin suna wahalar da jama’a wajen zagayawa.
These people speak 30 different languages, but use Russian as their lingua franca to communicate between each other.
Wadannan mutane suna magana da harsuna 30 daban-daban, amma suna amfani da Rashanci a matsayin harshensu don sadarwa tsakanin juna.￼
Most people of Dagestan are Muslims and belong to the Shafi`i school of Sunni Islam.
Mafi yawan mutane a Dagestan Musulmai ne waɗanda suke bin Sunnah a mazhabin Shai'iyya.

Gold is very soft.
Zinariya abu ce mai laushi sosai.
It is malleable, meaning a goldsmith can hammer it into thin metal sheets.
Yana da malle-lalle, ma'ana maƙerin zinare na iya tursasa shi cikin siraran ƙarfe.
It is also ductile, which means it can be pulled into wire.
Hakanan yana da ductile, wanda ke nufin ana iya ja shi cikin waya.
When it is used in money or in jewelry, it is often alloyed with silver or some other metal to make it harder.
Idan aka yi amfani da shi a cikin kuɗi ko kayan ado, sau da yawa ana haɗa shi da azurfa ko wani ƙarfe don yin tauri.
Most metals are gray in color.
Yawancin karafa suna da launin toka.
Gold is yellow because of the way its electrons behave.[1][2] The only other metal in common use that has a non-gray color is copper.
Zinariya rawaya ce saboda yadda electrons dinsa ke nuna hali. Wani ƙarfen da aka saba amfani da shi wanda ba shi da launin toka shine jan karfe.
Caesium also has a gold-like color, but it is not commonly used as a metal because it reacts with water.
Haka kuma Cesium yana da kala kamar zinare, amma ba a saba amfani da shi a matsayin karfe domin yana amsawa da ruwa.
Gold is a fairly good electrical conductor, better than aluminium, but not as good as copper or silver.
Zinariya ita ce jagorar wutar lantarki mai kyau, ta fi aluminum, amma ba ta kai tagulla ko azurfa ba.
Copper and brass electrical connectors, especially those used with computer and audio/video equipment, are often plated with gold for corrosion resistance.
Copper da tagulla masu haɗa wutar lantarki, musamman waɗanda aka yi amfani da su da kwamfuta da na'urorin sauti/bidiyo, galibi ana lulluɓe su da zinare don juriyar lalata.
Gold is also a good heat conductor, although it is not commonly used for this purpose due to its cost.
Zinariya kuma ita ce mai kula da zafi mai kyau, duk da cewa ba a saba amfani da shi don haka ba saboda tsadar sa.

Gold can mean that something or someone is very good or has done very well.
Zinariya na iya nufin cewa wani abu ko wani yana da kyau sosai ko kuma ya yi kyau sosai.
A gold medal is often the given to the first-place winner in a race or other sports.

Something that is in some way good may be given gold status.
Wani abu da yake a wata hanya mai kyau ana iya ba shi matsayin zinariya.
Safety
Kiyayewa
Metallic gold is non-toxic, which is unusual for a heavy metal.
Zinariya ta ƙarfe ba mai guba ba ce, wanda ba sabon abu bane ga ƙarfe mai nauyi.
Soluble gold compounds, however, are toxic to the liver and kidneys.
Abu bubuwan haɗin gwal masu narkewa, duk da tana, suna da guba ga hanta da koda.
Gold is non-flammable, even in a pure oxygen environment or when finely powdered.
Zinariya ba ta ƙonewa, ko da a cikin tsaftataccen yanayi na iskar oxygen ko kuma lokacin da aka feshe shi da kyau.
It does not react with most household or laboratory chemicals.
Ba ya amsa da mafi yawan sinadarai na gida ko dakin gwaje-gwaje.
