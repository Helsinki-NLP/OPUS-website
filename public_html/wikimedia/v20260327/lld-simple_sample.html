Little Alchemy is a puzzle game developed by Recloak Games, and designed by Jakub Koziol.
Storia
Gameplay In the game, you start with 4 elements, Water, Fire, Earth, and Air. The first game had around 100 elements to create, but since it has received multiple updates, it resulted in 589 elements so far. It was released first onto Google Chrome Webstore on December 8th 2010, where they later on expanded to other platforms, including making an official website for the game.
