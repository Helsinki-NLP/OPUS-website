 <s id="6">Ofishol Stetus</s>
 <s id="7">Dem Cook Ailen Māori esa Iistern Polynesian language.</s>
 <s id="8">Ets t' ofishol laenghwij o' dem Cook Ailen an esa indijenus laenghwij o' t' Realm o' Nyuu Ziilan.</s>
 <s id="9">Dem Cook Ailen Māori es rilii riilated t' Nyuu Ziilan Māori but es wan laenghwij.</s>
 <s id="10">Dem Cook Ailen Māori es simplii kauled Māori wen deir es no niid t' disambigyuait et f' Nyuu Ziilan Māori, but ets known as Māori Kūki 'Āirani (o Maori Kuki Airani), o, controvershulii, Rarotongan, tuu. Mani o' dem Cook Ailendas also kaul et Te reo Ipukarea, literalii "t' laenghwij o' t' Ansestral Hoemlan".</s>
 <s id="11">Dem Cook Ailen Māori b' kam a ofishol laenghwij o' dem Cook Ailen i' 2003,[6] but haufs no ofishol stetus i' Nyuu Ziilan, despiit t' fakt tat Nyuu Ziilan es signatorii t' t' Yunitid Nayshuns Declarayshun a' t' Riits o' Indijenus Piopels.</s>
 <s id="12">T' Te Reo Maori Act staits tat Māori:</s>
 <s id="13">(sii ekstirnal links).</s>
 <s id="14">Pukapukan es konsidired b' skolars an' talas tuu t' b' a distinkt laenghwij moor rilii riilated t' Sāmoan and Tokelauan t' Dem Cook Ailen Māori.</s>
 <s id="15">Et b'longs t' t' Samoic subgroup o' t' Polynesian laenghwij family.</s>
 <s id="16">T' intenshun b'hiind encludin' Pukapukan i' t' definishun o' Te Reo Maori wos t' meik shur o' ets protecshun.</s>
 <s id="17">T' dialekts[7] o' t' Iist Polynesian warietiis o' Dem Cook Ailen (colectivlii refirred t' as Dem Cook Ailen Māori) es:</s>
 <s id="18">Rakahanga-Manihiki Penrhyn (Tongarevan o Mangarongaro);[8] Sowthern: Rarotongan, Ngā Pū Toru (the dialects of Atiu, Mitiaro and Mauke), Aitutaki, Mangaia.</s>
 <s id="19">Dem Cook Ailen Māori es rilii relatid t' Tahitian an Nyuu Ziilan Māori, an deir es a degrii o' mushual intelijibilitii wit' both o' dese laenghwijs.</s>
 <s id="20">T' laenghwij es teoreticalii regulatid b' t' Kopapa Reo meik'd i' 2003, but dis organisayshun es currentlii dorment.</s>
 <s id="21">Deir esa debait o' t' stendardisayshun o' t' writin sistem.</s>
 <s id="22">Altho t' usaj o' t' macron (־) te makarona an t' glottel stawp amata (ꞌ) (/ʔ/) es recommendid, most talas don't use t' tuu diakritiks i' dail writin'.</s>
 <s id="23">Dem Cook Ailen Māori Revis'd Nyuu Testament uses a stendardis'd ortografii (spellin sistem) tat encludes t' diakritiks wen dey're fonemic but nor elsabout.</s>

 <s id="28">Dem Cook Ailen Māori es an isolatin laenghwij wit' a rilii smaal morfolojii.</s>
 <s id="29">Kaas es mark'd b' t' partikul tat inishiaits a nown frase, an lik most o' dem Iist Polynesian laenghwijs, dem Cook Ailen Māori haufs nominativ-accusativ kas markin.</s>
 <s id="30">T' unmark'd konstituent orduh es predicait inishul.</s>
 <s id="31">Tat es, werb inishul i' werbal frases an nominal-predicaot inishul i' non-werbal frases.</s>

 <s id="34">Fijian (Na Vosa Vakaviti) esa Austronesian laenghwij o' t' Malayo-Polynesian family tal'n b' soom 350 000–450 000 etnik Fijians as a nativ laenghwij.</s>
 <s id="35">T' 2013 Konstitushun meid Fijian as an ofishol laenghwij o' Fiji, wit' Inglish an Hindi tuu, an deir es diskushun abaut establishin et as t' "nashunal laenghwij", tho Inglish an Hindi wil stey ofishol.</s>
 <s id="36">Fijian esa VOS laenghwij.[3]</s>
 <s id="37">Stendard Fijian es bais'd a' t' talin' o' Bau, wich esa Iist Fijian laenghwij.</s>
 <s id="38">A pidginiz'd form es used b' mani Indo-Fijians an etnik Chinese a' dem ailen, whiil Pidgin Hindustani es used b' mani rural etnik Fijians.</s>

 <s id="60">Moriori esa[3] Polynesian laenghwij mos rilii riilated t' Nyuu Ziilan Māori.</s>
 <s id="61">Ets t' natiw laenghwij o' dem Moriori, t' endijenus piopel o' dem Chatham Ailen (Rēkohu i' Moriori) - an arkipelago o' Nyuu Ziilan, sowth iist o' Nyuu Ziilan's Sowth Ailen.</s>
 <s id="62">Dem Chatham Ailen's fas Uropean kontakt wos wit' William R. Broughton o' t' Yunitid Kingdum huu lan'd a' 29 Nowemba 1791 an klaim'd dem ailen.</s>
 <s id="63">Hii renam'd hiis shep, HMS Chatham.</s>
 <s id="64">Broughton's crewmen intermarrii'd wit' dem laaidiis o' Moriori.[4]</s>
 <s id="65">T' enwayshun f' Taranaki haufd a hewii empakt a' Moriori popyulayshun, kulchur an laenghwij, wit' onli 101 Moriori steyin i' 1862,[5] an few tal'n t' laenghwij b' t' 1870s.[6] Tho, Samuel Deighton, Resident Magistrate a' dem Chathams f' 1873 t' 1891, complii'd a short wokabyularii o' dem Moriori word, wit' deir ekwiwalents i' Māori an Inglish.</s>
 <s id="66">T' wokabyularii wos publish'd as an appendiks o' Michael King's Moriori: A Piopel Rediiscower'd.</s>
 <s id="67">T' jenosiid o' t' Moriori piopel okkur'd durin t' fawl o' 1835.</s>
 <s id="68">T' enwayshun o' dem Chatham Ailen left dem Moriori piopel an deir kulchur t' dy of.</s>
 <s id="69">Dos huu surwiiw'd wos iider kept as slaaws o eat'n an Moriori wos nor saankshun'd t' marrii otha Moriori o hauf children wit'i' deir raas.</s>
 <s id="70">Dis kaus'd deir piopel an deir laenghwij t' b' endanjer'd.</s>
 <s id="71">Deir wos onli 101 Moriori piopel left outta 2 000 huu haufd surwiiw'd i' 1863.[7]</s>
 <s id="72">T' laenghwij wos riimeik'd f' Barry Barclay's 2000 film dokumentarii T' Featas o' Piis,[8] i' a riimeikin o' Moriori kontakt wit' Pākehā an Māori.</s>
 <s id="73">I' 2001, as part o' a kulchural riiwiwal moowment, Moriori piopel b'gan attempts t' riiwiwe t' laenghwij, an kompiil'd a database o' dem Moriori word.[9] Deir esa POLLEX (Polynesian Lexicon Project Online) database o' dem Moriori word tuu.[10]</s>
 <s id="74">T' 2006 New Zealand census[4] show'd 945 piopel choosin t' include "Moriori" amonst deir triibal affiliayshuns, compar'd t' 35 piopel i' t' 1901 census.[11]</s>
 <s id="75">Wan hipotesis tat wos diskredited i' t' 1960s an 1970s staited dem Moriori piopel wos distinkt f' dem Māori set'las o' Nyuu Ziilan, an anotha hipotesis amon dem aarkaeolojists huu biiliew'd tat dem Moriori piopel huu set'l'd a' dem Chatham Ailen wos orijinalii Māori.[4]</s>
 <s id="76">T' jiiniaalojikel histrei o' t' Moriori laenghwij ekspooses t' Proto-tahitic subgroup tat separaits i't' dem for laenghwijs: Tahitian, Rarotongan, Tuamotuan, an Māori.</s>
 <s id="77">Moriori es konsidired a dialekt o' Maori.</s>
 <s id="78">Dem tuu laenghwij shar a riilated fonolojikal form.[12]</s>
