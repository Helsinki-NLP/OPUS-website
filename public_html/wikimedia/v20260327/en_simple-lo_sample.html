Creatine is used to produce adenosine triphosphate, ATP. ATP is the energy of the human body. Creatine is already in the body as a natural substance and in some food (for example beef).
ຄຣີອາຕິນ (ອັງກິດ: Creatine) ແມ່ນການປະສົມປະສານຂອງສາມອາຊິດອະມິໂນທີ່ບໍ່ຈໍາເປັນ. ເຫຼົ່ານີ້ແມ່ນ methionine, arginine ແລະ glycine. ຄຣີອາຕິນຖືກນໍາໃຊ້ເພື່ອຜະລິດ adenosine triphosphate, ATP.
It is then stored as energy in the muscles.[1] The energy systems of the body are: aerobic, lactate and phosphocreatine. Most of the creatine is used in the lactate system and the phosphocreatine system because these two systems need more energy than the aerobic system. They also take a part of the energy from the production or the absorption of creatine.

The Communist Party of the Russian Federation (CPRF; Russian: Коммунистическая партия Российской Федерации) is a Communist and Marxist–Leninist political party in Russia. It is the second largest political party in Russia. The party is often viewed as the successor of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU).

Provisional Revolutionary Government of the Republic of South Vietnam, also known as Republic of South Vietnam or PRG was a former country that existed in Asia from 1969 to 1976. It was established on 6 June, 1969 with the support of North Vietnam and Viet Cong. The PRG existed as an underground government to the Republic of Vietnam until 1975.
