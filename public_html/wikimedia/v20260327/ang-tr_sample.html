The people called Dadash are a Turkic people who have lived locally in Erzurum for a long time.The other meaning is those who perform local folkloric dances of Erzurum are called by this name.In addition, this word has many meanings in terms of the phonetic expansion of ancient names.“Theodosiopolis: Todo-si Polis" It is the ancient name of Erzurum.
1071 Malazgirt savaşının ardından Türkler 24 Oğuz boyu ile devletler, beylikler ve hanlıklar kurdular ve günümüze kadar dünyanın çeşitli yerlerine yayıldılar. Genel olarak Erzurum yöresinde 1- Erkek kardeş, 2- Yiğit, delikanlı, babayiğit kimse.
Dadasi/Dağ-atasi;in this respect,Dadashs are defined as mountaineers.There are approximately 1.5 million Dadash in Turkiye.Dadash people are originally from Kipchaks.It is believed that being a Dadash is an exceptional situation, both in Erzurum and its region and in Anatolia, and that it is a natural and noble state of mind in the sense of "superior people".These people are known in Turkiye for their extreme nationalism and religiousness, and are also known to be hospitable, amiable and helpful.It is very remarkable that the first President of the Republic of Turkiye, the Turkish politician and statesman Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, was elected as the President of the Assembly and Government as the Erzurum Deputy at the opening of the Turkish Grand National Assembly in Ankara on April 23, 1920.This event indicates how strongly the relation is between Atatürk and the Dadashs.
