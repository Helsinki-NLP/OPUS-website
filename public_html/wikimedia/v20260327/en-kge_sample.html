The dilruba (also spelled dilrupa)[1] is a bowed musical instrument originating in India.
Dilruba (tisabdu munih dilrupa)[1] joda da alat musik gisik say buasal jak India.

It is a type of Bowed Sitar that's slightly larger than an esraj and has a larger, square resonance box like a sarangi.
Ija joda da jenis Sitar Gisik say rada balak jak Esrangi rik uwat kutak resonansi pasagi say rada balak juk Sarangi.
The dilruba holds particular importance in Sikh history.
Dilruba uwat horti ponting khusus dilom sajarah Sikh.
It became more widely known outside India in the 1960s through use in songs by Western artists, such as the Beatles during their psychedelic phase (most notably in the song "Within You Without You").
Alat musik hasa mulai tikonal luwas di luwah India di tahun 1960-an ngaliyu'i pamakayan dilom lagu-lagu bak artis Barat, juk The Beatles sauni fase psikedelik tiyan (say paling nunjul dilom lagu "Within You Without You").

Donkervoort Automobielen BV is a manufacturer of hand-built and ultra light weight sports cars based in Lelystad, Netherlands.
Donkervoort Automobielen BV joda da produsen mubil sport rakitan pungu rik biyakna say hampang say bubasis di Lelystad, Balando.
The car brand was founded in 1978 by Joop Donkervoort.
Mirik mubil hasa titogakko di tahun 1978 bak Joop Donkervoort.
In 1996, Donkervoort's Ford engines were replaced by Audi engines.
Di tahun 1996, mosin Ford Donkervoort tigontiko rik mosin Audi.
Donkervoort's motto – "No Compromise" – means driving without any electronic aids such as ABS (anti-lock braking system), electronic stability program (ESP) or power steering.
Motto Donkervoort – "Makka Kompromi" – say hortina nyupir makka bantuan elektronik api-api juk ABS (sistim pangariman anti-lock), program stabilitas elektronik (ESP) atawa power steering.
