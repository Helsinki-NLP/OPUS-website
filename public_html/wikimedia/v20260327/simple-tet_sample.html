South Vietnam, officially the Republic of Vietnam was a country that existed from 1955 to 1975.
Vietname Súl, ofisialmente Repúblika Vietname (iha vietnam: Việt Nam Cộng hòa) maka nasaun ida ne'ebé eziste hosi tinan 1955 to'o 1975.

It occupied in the southern of what is now Southern Vietnam.
Nia okupa iha súl hosi saida maka agora hanaran Vietname Súl.
The country was south of the local Demilitarized Zone.
Nasaun ne'e iha súl hosi Zona Demilitarizada lokál.
