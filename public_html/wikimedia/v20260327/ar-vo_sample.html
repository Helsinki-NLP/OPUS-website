The wonderful Adventures of Nils**[1] (**ニルスのふしぎな旅**, **Nirusu no Fushigi na Tabi**) binom anime adapön dö novel fa 1906 **The Wonderful Adventures of Nils** fa svedik penül Selma Lagerlöf.[2] La 52 episo seiries binomofon in japonsik netvig **NHK** se januel 1980 ad march 1981.[1] Seiries binomom proköns primik fa **Pierrot**. Anime binomofom ad veritavik ad original, su difs at petik fa Nils, ek rolik lönöfik givon ad **Smirre** valan. Musäg binomom penön fa tsetik kompösör **Karel Svoboda**; **Yukihide Takekawa** pötom sonodram fa origina japonsik brodast e fa sötik votüds.
