COROT-Ekzo_7b Yer planetindən 489 işıq ili uzaqda olan, təkbuyunz bürcündəki COROT-7 ulduzunun ətrafında fırlanan ekzoplanetdir.
COROT-Exo-7b is an exoplanet that orbits the star COROT-Exo-7 in the constellation Monoceros, 489 light years from Earth.
İlk dəfə Fransa rəhbərliyindəki COROT missiyası tərəfindən aşkar edilmiş və 2009-cu ildə xəbəri verilmişdir. Kepler-10b-nin 2011-ci ilin yanvar ayında açıqlanmasına qədər, diyametr ölçüsü ilə ən kiçik ekzoplanet idi.
It was first detected by the French-led COROT mission and reported in 2009.[1] Until the announcement of Kepler-10b in January 2011, it was the smallest exoplanet to have its diameter measured, at 1.58 times that of the Earth which would give it a volume 3.95 times Earth's.
Planetin çox qıssa orbitə sahib olduğu üçün öz ulduzu ətrafında həddindən çox az vaxta öz dönüşünü tamamlayır hansı ki,öz ulduzu ətrafında bir tam dönüşü 20 saat bərabərdir. [1]
It was the first potential extrasolar terrestrial planet to be found. The planet is also notable for its very short orbital period: it revolves around its host star in about 20 hours.

Relationships were important in Morrissey's teachings, speaking of male and female characters.[51] Over the years Morrissey wrote articles for newspapers and magazines, writing on relationships.[52][3][4] In her book, No Less Than Greatness: Finding Perfect Love in Imperfect Relationships, Morrissey wrote about "relationship-building."(making friends) [54] The book was taught worldwide.[55][7] Writer Gary Zukav called the book "practical and inspiring,"[57] and writer Marianne Williamson wrote that the book "should be every couple's" friend(a guide for couples).[58] Robert LaCrosse wrote that No Less Than Greatness was a recommended resource in his book Learning From Divorce.[59] Writer Dennis Jones recommended the book in his 2008 book The Art of Being.[11].[60]

Morrissey used sentences from the Bible in her teaching,[1] as well as the Talmud,[53] the Tao Te Ching,[61] Thoreau[62] and others.

Wishing to show the New Thought movement in a simple nature, Morrissey wrote the book New Thought: A Practical Spirituality.
Kitab akademik iş üçün istifadə edilmişdir: Alternativ Psixoterapiyalar kitabında Jean Mercer onu "mənəvi dünya ilə əlaqəni" anlamaq üçün əsas kitab kimi qeyd etdi. Jones & Bartlett-in 2009-cu ildə nəşr etdirdiyi "Rəviyyat, Sağlamlıq və Şəfa" kitabında müəlliflər Young və Koopsen Morrissinin Yeni Düşüncəsinin Yeni Düşüncə hərəkatı ilə Yeni Əsr arasındakı fərqi anlamaq üçün vacib olduğunu yazaraq, "Yeni Düşüncə Yeni Əsr deyil" yazırdı. Oksford Universiteti Nəşriyyatının Müasir Yoqa Qurumu da daxil olmaqla bir çox tədqiqat kitabı Morrissinin Yeni Düşüncə kitabının Yeni Düşüncə qrupunu anlamağa kömək edən mühüm kitab olduğunu yazır.
Published by Penguin in 2002, the book was made of short essays from 40 New Thought leaders.[63] The book was used for academic work: in the book Alternative Psychotherapies Jean Mercer referred to it as a key book for understanding the "engagement with the spiritual world."[64] In Jones & Bartlett's 2009 book, Spirituality, Health, and Healing, authors Young and Koopsen wrote that Morrissey's New Thought was important for understanding the difference between the New Thought movement and New Age, writing that "New Thought is not New Age".[65] Many research books, including Oxford University Press' Gurus of Modern Yoga, write that Morrissey's New Thought book was an important book in helping to understand the New Thought group.[66][67]

Active from young age in international activism, Morrissey started the Association for Global New Thought in 1995 and was its first president.[1][10] In 1997 she worked with Mahatma Gandhi's grandson, Arun Gandhi, in starting the international Season for Nonviolence organization.[11][4] As of January 2019, Season for Nonviolence was celebrated around the world.[12]
Onun kitablarından götürülmüş cümlələr beynəlxalq jurnallarda, eləcə də kitablarda dərc olunub. Over the years Morrissey wrote articles for newspapers,[68] magazines,[2][3] and books.[69][5] She wrote for Success Magazine.[71] Sentences taken from her books were published in magazines internationally,[7][73] as well as in books.[74][75] Her writings are written in self-help books,[76][77][78] Christian teachings books,[79][80][81] books about rights,[82][83][84] work,[85][86] and happiness.[87][88] Simon & Schuster's Chicken Soup for the Soul series sometimes open chapters with her sentences.[89][90]
