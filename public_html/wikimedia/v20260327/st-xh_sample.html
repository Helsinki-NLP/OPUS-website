Cape Town qalong e ile ya hana ho kengwa tshebetsong ha Group Areas Act e fetisitsweng ka 1950, mme dibaka tsa bodulo toropong di ile tsa dula di sa arololwa ho fihlela dibaka tsa pele tsa dihlopha di phatlalatswa toropong ka 1957.
Ekuqaleni iKapa lachasa ukuphunyezwa koMthetho weMimandla yeQela owapasiswa ngo-1950, kwaye iindawo zokuhlala esixekweni zahlala zingahlulwanga de kwabhengezwa iMimandla yeQela yokuqala esixekweni ngo-1957.
Qetellong ha Motse Kapa o qala ho kenya tshebetsong Molao wa Mabaka a Dihlopha, o ile wa etsa ka matla ho feta motse ofe kapa ofe o moholo; bohareng ba dilemo tsa bo-1980, e ne e se e fetohile e nngwe ya metse e arohaneng ka ho fetisisa Afrika Borwa.
Xa ekugqibeleni iKapa laqalisa ukusebenzisa uMthetho weMimandla yeQela, lenza ngokuqatha kakhulu kunaso nasiphi na esinye isixeko esikhulu; phakathi kwiminyaka yee-1980, yaba sesinye sezona zixeko zicalulwayo eMzantsi Afrika.
Merero ya ho aha Khayelitsha e phatlaladitswe la pele ke Ngaka Piet Koornhof ka 1983, eo ka nako eo e neng e le Letona la Tshebedisano-mmoho le Ntshetsopele.
Izicwangciso zokwakha iKhayelitsha zabhengezwa okokuqala nguGq Piet Koornhof ngo-1983, owayenguMphathiswa weNtsebenziswano noPhuhliso ngoko.
Ka 1985, toropo ya Site C e ne e na le baahi ba 30,000.
Ngo-1985, ihlomela-dolophu leSite C lalinabantu abangama-30,000.
Khayelitsha e ne e le e nngwe ya matsapa a ho qetela a mmuso wa kgethollo a ho phethahatsa Molao wa Mabaka a Dihlopha mme e ile ya bonwa e le tharollo mathateng a mabedi: palo e ntseng e hola ka potlako ya bafalli ba tswang Kapa Botjhabela le tshubuhlellano ya makeisheneng a mang Motse Kapa. [1]
IKhayelitsha yayiyenye yeenzame zokugqibela zorhulumente wocalu-calulo 8zokunyanzelisa umthetho weGroup Areas Act kwaye yabonwa njengesisombululo kwiingxaki ezimbini: inani elikhula ngokukhawuleza labaphambukeli abasuka eMpuma Koloni kunye nokuxinana kwezinye iilokishi zaseKapa.[2]
Kgethollo le taolo ya baahi ba batsho ke mmuso wa kgethollo ha di a ka tsa thibela batho ba batsho ho aha mahaeng a Motse Kapa.
Ucalucalulo nokulawulwa kwabemi abantsundu ngurhulumente wocalucalulo akuzange kubathintele abantu abantsundu ukuba bazinze emaphandleni aseKapa.
Kamora ho fediswa ha melao ya dipasa ka 1987, batho ba bangata ba batsho, haholo Maxhosa, ba ile ba fallela dibakeng tse potileng Motse Kapa ho ya batla mesebetsi.
Emva kokuchithwa kwemithetho yamapasi ngo-1987 uninzi lwabantu abamnyama, ingakumbi amaXhosa, bafudukela kwimimandla engqonge iKapa befuna imisebenzi.
Ka nako eo, batho ba batsho ba bangata ba ne ba se ba ahile ka ntle ho molao makeisheneng a kang Nyanga le Crossroads. [1] Ka 1983 le 1984, maemo a mekhukhung a kang Crossroads le KTC a ile a mpefala mme a mpefatswa ke lerumo la mapolesa moo matlo a ileng a heletswa le ho hlaha ha Witdoeke, e etelletsweng pele ke "Mayor" Johnson Ngxobongwana.
Ngelo xesha, uninzi lwabantu abantsundu lwalusele luzinze ngokungekho mthethweni kwiilokishi ezifana neNyanga neCrossroads.[3]Ngo-1983 no-1984, iimeko kwiinkampu zamatyotyombe ezifana ne-Crossroads kunye ne-KTC zaba mandundu kwaye zenziwa mandundu ngumgaqo-nkqubo osemthethweni wamapolisa apho amakhaya ayetshatyalalisiwe kunye nokuvela kwe-Witdoeke, ekhokelwa ngu-"Sodolophu" uJohnson Ngxobongwana.
Ma-Witdoekes a ile a tshehetswa haholo ke mmuso wa kgethollo ntweng ya ona kgahlano le UDF e ikamahanyang le ANC, e neng e hanyetsa pepenene merero ya ho fallisetsa batho lekeisheneng le letjha la Khayelitsha.
AmaWitdoeke ayexhaswa kakhulu ngurhulumente wocalucalulo kumlo wakhe owawuchasene ne-UDF eyayamene neANC, neyayichasa ngokuphandle izicwangciso zokuba abantu bafuduselwe kwilokishi entsha yaseKhayelitsha.
Ha palo ea batho ba batšo e ntse e eketseha, ’muso oa apartheid o ile oa batla ho rarolla “bothata” boo ka ho theha dibaka tse ncha tseo batho ba batsho ba ka phelang ho tsona.
Njengoko abantu abantsundu besanda, urhulumente wocalucalulo wafuna ukusombulula “ingxaki” ngokuseka iindawo ezintsha ezihlala abantu abantsundu.
Khayelitsha e thehilwe ka 1985 mme palo e kgolo ya batho e ile ya fallisetswa moo ka dikgoka, haholo ka kgotso empa ka dinako tse ding e le mabifi. [2]
IKhayelitsha yasekwa ngo-1985 kwaye inani elikhulu labantu lafuduswa apho ngenkani, ubukhulu becala ngoxolo kodwa ngamanye amaxesha ngobundlobongela.[4]
Isivivana ke setsi se fumaneha Khayelitsha mme ke tulo ya batjha eo ho yona ho nang le tulo ya ho bala,ho shebella ditori, dikomporo le tse ding.
