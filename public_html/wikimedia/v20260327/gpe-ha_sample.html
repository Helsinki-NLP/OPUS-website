T.I. Ahmadiyya Senior High School (Real Amass) is a coeducational second-cycle public educational institution in Kumasi in the Ashanti Region of Ghana.
Makarantar T.I. Ahmadiyya Senior High School (Real Amass) makaranta ce ta jinsi biyu ta jama'a a Kumasi a yankin Ashanti na Ghana.

Dem establish the school in de year 30th January,1950 .De first Headmaster of de School was Dr. S. B. Ahmad, an expatriate, wey start de school with twenty-five students and seven teachers.[1]
An kafa makar antar a shekarar 30 ga Janairu, 1950. Shugaban makarantar na farko shine Dr. S. B. Ahmad, wani mai gudun hijira, wanda ya fara makarantar tare da dalibai 25 da malamai 7.
