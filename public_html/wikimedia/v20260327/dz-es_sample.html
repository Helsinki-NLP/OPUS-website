Poybo Media is a Canadian digital media company that produces social content under its wholly-owned Internet properties.[1] It was founded in May 2020 by Justin Jin.[2] As of July 2023, the company's media brands attracted a total viewership of more than 1.5 billion across social platforms.[3]
