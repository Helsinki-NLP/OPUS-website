In Myanmar, vegetable cultivation is an essential aspect of agriculture, playing a significant role in ensuring food security and supporting the livelihoods of many rural communities. The country's diverse geographical regions provide a conducive environment for growing a wide variety of vegetables.
Marvel Studios releases its films in groups called "Phases", with the first three phases collectively known as "The Infinity Saga" and the following three phases as "The Multiverse Saga".
Traditional agricultural practices, including both rain-fed and irrigated farming, are prevalent across the nation.
The first MCU film, Iron Man (2008), began Phase One, which culminated in the 2012 crossover film The Avengers.
Farmers often employ age-old techniques passed down through generations, combined with modern agricultural methods and equipment to enhance productivity.
Phase Two began with Iron Man 3 (2013) and concluded with Ant-Man (2015).
During the monsoon season, when rainfall is abundant, crops like rice, peas, and leafy greens flourish.
Phase Three began with Captain America: Civil War (2016) and concluded with Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019).
In drier regions, vegetables such as tomatoes, cucumbers, and eggplants thrive thanks to carefully managed irrigation systems.
Phase Four began with Black Widow (2021) and concluded with Black Panther: Wakanda Forever (2022).
While small-scale family farms dominate vegetable cultivation, larger commercial enterprises are also emerging, contributing to the nation's agricultural development.
Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania (2023) began Phase Five, which will end with Blade (2025), and Phase Six will begin with Fantastic Four (2025).
The cultivation of vegetables not only sustains Myanmar's food supply but also generates income opportunities and supports rural communities in their pursuit of a sustainable future.
Phase Six and "The Multiverse Saga" will conclude with Avengers: The Kang Dynasty (2026) and Avengers: Secret Wars (2027).

背景 《豬囝佩奇》每蜀集大概5分鐘，講有蜀頭豬，名「佩奇」，是諸娘囝。伊共伊厝裏儂、客佻幫發生了野価事計。伊其朋友，都是各種各色其哺乳類動物，比例講貓囝、犬囝、兔囝等等，並且都共伊蜀樣大。伊其依弟「喬治」也有另蜀堆共伊蜀樣大其客佻幫。故事其內容，大部份是圍繞平常其生活，比例伲囝哥參加其學前遊戲班、泅水、去依公依嬤厝或且表親其厝、著遊樂場客佻、騎骹踏車等等。 動畫裏勢其角色，雖然講是動物，都共儂蜀樣，有衣裳頌，有厝住，有其也會仈開車，但同時固保留動物其特性。例如，佩奇共伊厝裏儂講話其前後，時不時會像豬蜀樣𧲇𧲇叫。其他動物角色也會發出自家其叫聲。有其固會展現其他其特性，比例兔囝其角色，除𡅏講話其時候會「嗚咿嗚咿」叫，也盡去愛食紅蘿蔔。有仂囝例外其是，兔囝住著山懸頂其洞洞裏勢，雖然伊各儂其洞洞都像人類其厝蜀樣有檻門囝共家具。各種角色無面、畏羞其時候，面都會變紅，喙也有能耐表達悲傷、歡喜、受氣、困惑等等情緒。雖然動畫裏勢其哺乳類動物都有擬人化，但是其他其動物就無總款了。比例鴨囝、烏龜小提、鸚鵡Polly、金魚小金等等。動畫其旁白由John Sparke負責，伊其旁白加強了劇情其喜劇效果：比例有什乇𣍐好其事計發生（比例喬治啼嘛），伊就會加蜀句「啊呀，仱死了」；或者如果角色做其事計野危險（比例佩奇騎車無看前斗），伊就大告「著細膩」。
