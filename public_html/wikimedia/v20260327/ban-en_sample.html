St. Joseph Higher Secondary School and College (Bengali: সেন্ট যোসেফ উচ্চ মাধ্যমিক বিদ্যালয়), also known as SJSC is an American Catholic higher secondary school and college in Asad Gate, Mohammadpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh.
Kasengguh pinaka silih tunggil perguruan tinggi sané pinih utama ring Bangladesh.[1] Puniki sekolah moned (boys') sané ngicénin pendidikan saking kelas tiga ngantos kelas dua belas, rata-rata umur 8-18 warsa.
Widely regarded as one of the greatest college in Bangladesh.[1] It is a mono-ed (boys') school offering education from Third grade to Twelfth, averaging 8–18 years of age.
Sekolah punika wantah sekolah tunggal (pagi) antuk langkung saking 10 ewu sisia.[2]
The school is a single-shift (morning) school with over 10 thousand students.[2]
Sasampun kamerdékaan Bangladesh kaentosin saking basa Inggris dados basa Bengali. Mangkin, basa Inggris miwah basa Bengali kaanggén ring pendidikan ring tingkat menengah.[1] Saking aran "Sekolah Tinggi St. Joseph" pendidikan sampun kawedar ring tingkat menengah sane tegeh. Nyabran warsa 650-750 siswa lulus saking cabang perguruan tinggi lan 165 siswa saking cabang sekolah.
After the independence of Bangladesh it was changed from English to Bengali medium. At present, English and Bengali mediums are used for teaching at the secondary level.[1] Since the name "St. Joseph's Higher Secondary School" education has been imparted here at the higher secondary level. Every year 650-750 students pass out from its college branch and 165 students from the school branch.
Siswa saking institusi puniki kauningin dados "Josephites".[2]
The students of this institution are known as "Josephites".[2]
Saking warsa 1960-an, Sekolah Menengah Tinggi St. Joseph polih reputasi pinaka lembaga pendidikan menengah sane pinih becik ring negara.
Since the 1960s, St. Joseph's Higher Secondary School gained a reputation as the best institution for higher secondary education in the country.
