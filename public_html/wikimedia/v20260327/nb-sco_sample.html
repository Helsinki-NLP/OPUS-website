Xenaroswelliana deltaquadrant is a bogle o’ a runnin’ beetle – the yin an’ only o’ its kin in the hale sleekit (genus Xenaroswelliana). It bides in its ain wee unner-faimlie (subfaimily Xenaroswellianinae), deep in the labyrinth o’ the Carabidae faimlie. This bizzum’s kent frae but ae sowel (thon holotype, a chiel), grippit i’ the Amazonian wids o’ Brasil back in ’74.

Utseende
Sicht
En ganske liten (holotypen er 4,6 millimeter lang), blekt gulbrun løpebille.
A wee bit runnin beetle (thon holotype’s nae mair nor 4.6mm lang), peely-wally broon-yella.
Hode og kropp er temmelig jevnbrede, pronotum firkantet.
Baith heid an’ body near-enough flat as a bannock, wi’ a square-cuttit pronotum.
Dekkvingene er tvert avskåret bakerst.
The wing-sheaths is hackit aff sharp at the airse-end.
Hodet har spredte, lange hår.
The heid’s scrievit oot wi lang-shankit hairs.
