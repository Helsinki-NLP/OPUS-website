Hadiza Isma El-Rufai (an haife ta a 21 ga watan Yuni, 1960) Yar Najeriya ce, kuma marubuciya, wanda itace uwargidan gwamnan jihar Kaduna Malam Nasir El-Rufai.
Hadiza Isma El-Rufai (ti a bi ni June 21, 1960) jẹ onkọwe ati onkọwe ọmọ ilu Naijiria, iyawo ti gomina ipinlẹ Kaduna, Malam Nasir El-Rufai .

Hadiza ce ta kafa cibiyar nan na Yasmin El-Rufai Foundation (YELF), cibiya dake maida hankali gurin koyar da ilimi ga mata da kananan yara, musamman rubutun kirkira da nazari.
Hadiza ipilẹ ti Yasmin El-Rufai Foundation (YELF), ile-iṣẹ ti a ṣe iyasọtọ lati pese eto-ẹkọ si awọn obinrin ati awọn ọmọde, pataki kikọ ati iwadi.
Helon Habila ya bayyana aikin marubuciyar amatsayin, "wani labari mai ratsa zuciya akan rashi da kuma zaburarwa akan karfin mace da jajircewan ta" wato (a turanci "a heartbreaking tale of loss and an uplifting story of a woman's strength and determination").[7][8]
Helon Abel ṣapejuwe iisee onkọwe, “ itan itanjẹ pipadanu ati ibanujẹ lori agbara ati ifaramo obirin ” (ni Gẹẹsi, “itan itan pipadanu ati itan akin ti agbara ati ipinnu obirin” ). [1] [2]
Tana da BSc da MSc a Architecture (1983) da kuma MBA (1992) duk daga Jami'ar Ahmadu Bello, Zaria, sannan tasamu Masters a Rubutun Kirkira (Creative Writing) (2012) daga Jami'ar Bath Spa, United Kingdom.[2]
O ni o ni a BSc ati MSC ni faaji (1983) ati da MBA (1992) lati Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, ati ki o wulo Masters ni Ṣẹda Text (Creative kikọ) (2012) lati awọn University of Bath Spa, United Kingdom. [1]
Daga nan ne, tafara aiki amatsayin malamar jami'a a sashen koyar da zane-zane (Architecture) a Kaduna Polytechnic, sannan tayi aiki a hukumar wutan lantarki ta kasa wato (National Electric Power Authority, NEPA), kafin nan tazama ma'aikaciya mai zaman kanta.
Lati ki o si lori, ti o ba bẹrẹ ṣiṣẹ ni kilasi University olukọni ni Eka kọni awọn ona (Architecture) ni Kaduna Polytechnic, ati lati ise lori awọn ẹrọ itanna ọkọ ni isalẹ (awọn National Electric Power Authority, NEPA), nipa tazama abáni olominira.
El-Rufai najin harsuna uku Hausa, Turanci da Faransanci.
El-Rufai nfuni ni awọn ede mẹta: Gẹẹsi, Gẹẹsi ati Faranse .
A 2017, El-Rufai ta wallafa littafinta ''An Abundance of Scorpions'' (Ouida Books).[4] littafin nobel ne, wanda tasamu kwarin-gwiwa daga aikin data taba yi na sakai a wani gidan marayu dake babban birni Abuja, da kuma Son ta na tayi rubutu akan maraya.[5] An gabatar da littafin a Aké Arts and Book Festival na shekarar 2017.[6]
Ni ọdun 2017, El-Rufai ṣe atẹjade iwe rẹ An Abundance of Scorpions (Anwọn iwe Ouida). [1] O jẹ ijẹrisi ti Nobel, lati eyiti a ṣe iwuri awokose latiiṣẹ iṣaaju ni ibi itọju alainibaba ni olu-ilu Abuja, atipee Ọmọ naa kọwe lori awọn alainibaba. [2] Iwenaa ni a gbekalẹ n Aké Arts ati Book Festival of 2017.
