Jörg Immendorff (14 de làmpadas de su 1945 - 28 de maju de su 2007) est istadu unu pintore, scultore e professore de arte tedescu.
Jörg Immendorff (14 June 1945 – 28 May 2007) was a German painter, sculptor and stage designer. He was also art professor.

Est istadu cunsideradu un'artista neo-espressionista.[1][2]
He was considered a neo-expressionist artist.[1][2]
A s'ispissu aiat creadu "cìrculos mannos de pinturas" de natura polìtica, a bortas ispirados dae Sa Germània moderna.
He often created "grand cycles of paintings" of political nature, sometimes inspired by modern Germany, that often took him several years to complete.
Sos ciclos suos prus famosos incluent Cafè Deutschland e The Rake's Progress.[1]
His most famous cycles included Cafè Deutschland, and The Rake's Progress.[3]
