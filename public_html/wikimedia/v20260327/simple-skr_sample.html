Sonya Dunbar (born March 31, 1969) is an American dental hygienist based in Jacksonville.[1][2] She is also known as Geriatric Tooth Fairy.[3][…]

Career Dunbar is a co-founder and vice president of Mobile Dental Xpress. She also serves on the board of Tooth Cloud, an organization that supports oral health technology initiatives. In addition to her clinical work, Dunbar has presented at TEDx Talk titled “How to Save Our Seniors with Just a Simple Toothbrush”.[1] She is also associated with the American Mobile & Teledentistry Alliance and the National Mobile & Teledentistry Conference.[2][3][4] References
