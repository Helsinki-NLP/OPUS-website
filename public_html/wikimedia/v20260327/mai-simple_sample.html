Arvind Otta is an Indian psychologist, author, and editor of Psychologs magazine.[1] He is known for the rights of people suffering from mental health illnesses.[2][3] He did Bachelor of Psychology from Jamia Millia Islamia and postgraduate and PhD from Ambedkar University.[4][5]
