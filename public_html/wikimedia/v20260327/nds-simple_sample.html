Ionuț Silaghi de Oaș, Graf de Szilágyi-Oaș[1] (geboren im 16 März 1990) ist ein rumänischer Sänger, Songwriter,[2][3][4][5][6] Produzent,[7] Autor, Aristokrat, Unternehmer[8] und Ingenieur.[9] Er ist das derzeitige Oberhaupt der Adelsfamilie Szilágyi-Oaș und der derzeitige Generalsekretär von Ansamblul Oaşul.[10][11][12] Er ist Absolvent der Technischen Universität von Cluj-Napoca und der Babeş-Bolyai-Universität.
Ionuț Silaghi de Oaș, Count de Szilágyi-Oaș[1] (born March 16, 1990) is a Romanian singer, songwriter,[2][3][4][5][6] producer,[7] collector of folk music, author, aristocrat, entrepreneur[8] and engineer.[9] He is the current head of the Szilágyi-Oaș noble family and the current Secretary General of Ansamblul Oaşul.[10][11][12] He is a graduate of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca and of the Babeş-Bolyai University.

Er absolvierte auch einen Master-Abschluss an der Technischen Universität von Cluj-Napoca.[1][13]
He also completed a master's degree at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca.[1][13]

Behavior (or behaviour) is what an animal does and how they act.
Bedregen oder Verholen is wat Dere oder Minschen doot.
Behaviors may be conscious or unconscious.

The term is not well-defined.[1]
In Experimenten könnt Forscherlüde Bedregen as ene Reaktschoon van enen Organismus up enen Stimulus bekieken.
In experiments, behavior is the observed reaction which occurs when an organism is given a stimulus.
Welk Organismen sind komplexer as ander un neemt dat Ümmefeld bewusster waar.
Some organisms are more complicated than others and may be more aware of their surroundings.
Wenn dat Bedregen bewusst is, heet dat dat se weet wat se doot.
If their behavior is conscious it means that they know what they are doing.
Wenn dat Bedregen unbewusst is, bedüdt dat, dat se nich weet wat se doot.
If they are not aware of what they are doing this is unconscious behavior.
Minschen treckt de Hand troügge, wenn se wat heets anraket un denket daar nich över na.
Humans will pull their hand back without thinking if they accidentally touch something hot.
Dat passeert, ümme dat wi un ook ander Deren Reflexe in use Nervensysteem hebbet, de uus straks de Hand wegtrecken maket Pien aftowenden.
This is done because we (and other animals) inherit nervous system reflexes which cause us to pull away from the thing that is causing the pain.

Behavior is linked to the nervous system as well as the endocrine system.
Dat Nervensysteem reageert un nimt waar wat passeert.
The nervous system reacts, and notices what happens.
Dat Hormoonsysteem is langsamer un maket vescheden Aarden Bedregen waarschienliker oder minner waarschienlik.
The hormone system is much slower. It makes some kinds of behavior more, or less, likely.

A simple example is the change from being a child to an adult.
Daar speelt vele verscheden Hormone enr Rulle bi, de up Wassdoom man ook up dat Bedregen Inflood neemt.
This involves a whole range of hormones which affect growth and behavior.
Bedregen hänget ook den Leren tohope.
Behavior is also linked to learning.

Organisms which are more complicated can learn better than those that are simpler.

For organisms, learning means that they change their behavior because they remember having been in that situation before and know how to react.

People often use the word "behavior" to mean the way people interact with (behave towards) one another.

Good behavior is about being polite and thoughtful to other people.
