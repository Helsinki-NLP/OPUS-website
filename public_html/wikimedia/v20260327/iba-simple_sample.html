Solána Imani Rowe (ada kena 8 November 1989[1]) dikelala enggau nama SZA, iya nya siku pemuzik R&B ari menua Amerika.
Solána Imani Rowe (born November 8, 1989[2]) known professionally as SZA, is an American R&B musician.

Iya ada ba Louis, Missouri, lalu diau ba Maplewood, New Jersey maya iya mit suba.
She was born in St. Louis, Missouri, and raised in Maplewood, New Jersey.
Iya berengkah ngaga muzik ba pun taun 2010-an.
She began making music in the early 2010s.
Lebuh iya ngepunka karier muzik iya, iya ngaga dua iti EP.
When she started her music career, she made two EPs.
Udah nya, iya nyain kontrak enggau label rikod hip hop Top Dawg Entertainment.
After that, she signed with the hip hop record label Top Dawg Entertainment.
Nengah pengawa nyain sempekat nya, SZA ngeluarka EP studio ketiga iya, Z, dalam taun 2014.[2] Dalam taun ke sama, iya sama nulis "Feeling Myself" enggau Nicki Minaj sereta Beyoncé.
Through the signing, SZA released her third studio EP, Z, in 2014.[3] During that same year, she co-wrote "Feeling Myself" with Nicki Minaj and Beyoncé.
Dalam taun 2016, iya bisi dipandangka dalam lagu Rihanna "Consideration".
In 2016, she was featured in Rihanna's song "Consideration".
Dalam taun 2017, iya ngeluarka album studio keterubah iya, Ctrl.
In 2017, she released her first studio album, Ctrl.
Album studio nya manggai ba lumur 3 ba Billboard 200.
It reached number 3 on the Billboard 200.
Iya diberi setipikit tiga kali platinum ari Gerempung Industri Rikod Amerika (RIAA).
It was certified triple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
Iya dikeluarka enggau puji entarabansa ari bala kritik.[1]
It was released to international praise from critics.[4]
Maya taun 2017, iya bekerejasama enggau Maroon 5 ngaga single hit Top 10 "What Lovers Do".
Also in 2017, she collaborated with Maroon 5 to make the Top 10 hit single "What Lovers Do".
Taun ke nangkanka nya, ba taun 2018, iya bekerejasama enggau Kendrick Lamar ngaga single Top 10 "All The Stars" ungkup lagu Black Panther.
The next year, in 2018, she collaborated with Kendrick Lamar to create the Top 10 single "All The Stars" for the Black Panther soundtrack.
"All The Stars" dichalunka dalam Oscar ungkup kededua artis nya.[1]
"All The Stars" was nominated for an Oscar for both artists.[5]
Dalam taun 2020, iya mansutka single "Good Days".
In 2020, she released the single "Good Days".
Dalam taun 2021, iya ngeluarka single "I Hate U" lalu bekerejasama enggau Doja Cat ngaga lagu "Kiss Me More".
In 2021, she released the single "I Hate U" and collaborated with Doja Cat to make the song "Kiss Me More".
Ketetiga lagu single nya manggai ba penuduk 10 pemadu manah ba Billboard Hot 100 Amerika Syarikat.[1][2] Dalam taun 2022, SZA ngeluarka album studio kedua iya, "SOS".
All three singles reached the top 10 on the US Billboard Hot 100.[6][7] In 2022, SZA released her second studio album, "SOS".
Album SOS manggai ba lumur satu ba Billboard 200.[3] Iya mega bisi ayan ba Randau Setengah Jam Super Bowl 2025[4], maya pengawa pemerindang Kendrick Lamar, mantaika lagu Luther enggau All The Stars.
SOS reached number one on the Billboard 200.[8] She also appeared in the 2025 Super Bowl Halftime Show[9], during the performance by Kendrick Lamar, preforming the songs Luther and All The Stars.
Anugerah enggau dichalun
Awards and nominations
SZA udah bulih mayuh bengkah anugerah nyengkaum empat iti Anugerah Grammy, Anugerah Muzik Billboard, Anugerah Muzik Amerika enggau dua Anugerah Muzik Video MTV enggau nominasyen ungkup Anugerah Akademi enggau Anugerah Golden Globe.
SZA has earned several awards including four Grammy Award, a Billboard Music Award, an American Music Award and two MTV Video Music Award with nominations for an Academy Award and a Golden Globe Award.
