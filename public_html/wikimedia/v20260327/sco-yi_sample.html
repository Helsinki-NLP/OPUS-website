Black September ( ) was a Palestinian terrorist group, whose name comes from an event that took place in Jordan in September 1970, when Hussein King of Jordan ordered the Jordanian army to put down an uprising. And also as revenge in an assassination attempt, which the Palestine Liberation Organization tried to carry out against him. Her main activities were in the 1970s.
