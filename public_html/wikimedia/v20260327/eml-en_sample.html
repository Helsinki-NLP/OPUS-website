Kyriakos Mitsotakis (Greek: Κυριάκος Μητσοτάκης, Kyriákos Mitsotákis [ciɾˈʝako̞s̠ mit͡so̞ˈtacis̠]; born 4 March 1968) is a Greek politician who has been serving as the prime minister of Greece from June 2023.

U so babbu, Konstantinos Mitsotakis, chì campatu da u 1918 à u 2017, era ancu u Primu Ministru di u paese da u 1990 à u 1993.
He was also the prime minister of Greece from July 2019 to May 2023.
Mitsotakis hà rializatu à a dirigenza di u partitu di a Nova Democrazia in l'alizzioni di ghjennaghju 2016 è era u capu di l'uppusizione in u Parlamentu ellenicu finu à a so vittoria assai contestata in l'elezzioni generali di u 2019, scunfittu u primu ministru in carica Alexis Tsipras.
On 25 June 2023, Mitsotakis won a second term as prime minister after a historic victory for his New Democracy party,[1] for which he has served as president since 2016.
Ripeterà u so trionfo in l'elezzioni generale di u 2023 di maghju è di ghjugnu, scunfiggendu u so rivalu Tsipras da una larga margine è assicurendu un guvernu supermajorità.
Mitsotakis previously was Leader of the Opposition from 2016 to 2019, and Minister of Administrative Reform from 2013 to 2015.
U so tempu in u putere hè stata carattarizata da un sconvolgimentu in e fragili finanze di a Grecia dopu à i 10 anni di a crisa ecunomica greca chì hà lasciatu u paese in lotta è a miseria generalizata, a risposta di u so guvernu à a pandemia di COVID-19 è una forte pulitica estera.
He is the son of the late Konstantinos Mitsotakis, who served as Prime Minister of Greece from 1990 to 1993. He was first elected to the Hellenic Parliament for the Athens B constituency in 2004.
Tuttavia, hà affruntatu critichi per a suppressione di a libertà di stampa è di i media, i spintamenti di migranti da u Mari Mediterraniu, u scandalu di l'interceptazioni di u 2022, chì hà espostu l'spionamentu da u guvernu versu i pulitici è i funzionari è a mala gestione di diverse crisi interne, cum'è u trenu. crash à la Vallée de Tempi le 1er mars 2023.
After New Democracy suffered two election defeats in 2015, he was elected the party's leader in January 2016. Three years later, he led his party to a majority in the 2019 Greek legislative election.

Gamma mapa de l'airal de la resèrva de Pilibhit La resèrva dels tigres de Pilibhit es situada dins la region de Terai d'Uttar Pradesh, Índia. Foguèt establida coma una resèrva de tigres en 2014, cobrís una superfícia d'aperaquí 730 quilomètres carrats, s'espandissent dins los districtes de Pilibhit, e Shahjahanpur. La resèrva fa partida del païsatge de l'arc Terai mai grand, un abitat critic que religa Índia e Nepal e sosten una gama divèrsa de flòra e fauna.
Pilibhit Tiger Reserve (pronounced [piːliːbʱiːt̪ ˈtaɪɡə rɪˈzɜːvː]), is among the narrowest yet most canopy-rich tiger reserves, spread across a horseshoe-shaped landscape.[7] The reserve is known to have an estimated tiger density of 10.8 per 100 km2 area.[c] The reserve covers a total area of 730.24 km2, of which 97.6% (712.88 km2) lies in Pilibhit district and 2.4% (17.36 km2) in Shahjahanpur district, both located in the Rohilkhand region of Uttar Pradesh.[8][9] Approximately 21% of Pilibhit district's total geographical area comes under reserve's limits, thereby positioning it in the category of Uttar Pradesh's most forest-rich districts.
Bordat pel riu Sharda al nòrd-èst e lo riu Ghaghara al sud-oèst, lo païsatge de Pilibhit es caracterizat per de bòsques de Sal exuberants, de pradas nautas, de paluns e de planas d'inondacion fertilas. L'ostal del majestuós tigre de Bengala, la resèrva abriga tanben de leopards, de cèrvis de palun, de sanglièrs, e mai de 300 espècias d'aucèls, çò que ne fa un paradís pels apassionats de la vida salvatja e los observators d'aucèls. La mescla unica de bòsques e de zònas umidas de Pilibhit provesís de terrens de reproduccion ideals pels tigres e d'autres grands mamifèrs.
It consists of the Himalayan lowlands terrain along the Indo–Nepal border in the upper northern plains.
En 2020, foguèt onorat amb lo "Prèmi TX2" per aver doblat sa populacion de tigres en un decenni — un testament de sos esfòrces de conservacion capitats. Uèi, la resèrva dels tigres de Pilibhit es un modèl de conservacion de la biodiversitat, en equilibrant la preservacion ecologica amb lo desvolopament comunitari sostenible.
The habitat is distinguished by tall grasslands, sal forests, and the swamp ecosystem, which is maintained through regular flooding events originating from nearby rivers, riverlets and lakes during the rainy seasons. The Sharda sagar dam elongating up to a length of 22 km (14 mi) is on the edge of the reserve.[10]
