Kimenyi Yves is a Rwandan professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Rwanda Premier League club AS Kigali FC. Throughout his career he has played for teams like Rayon Sports and APR FC who are both first division top teams in the Rwanda Premier League, he also plays for the Rwandan national football team Amavubi.
Kimenyi Yves ni umukinnyi w'umupira w'amaguru wa Kiyovu Sports akaba ari umuzamu, yakiniye amakipe nka Rayon Sports na APR FC zose zo mu cyiciro cya mbere muri shampiyona y’u Rwanda akaba kandi ari umuzamu w'ikipe yigihugu amavubi, Ni mu gihe nyampinga Muyango Uwase ariwe mugore we yamenyekanye ubwo yitabiraga irushanwa ry’ubwiza rya Miss Rwanda mu 2019.

Kimenyi Yves is married to Muyango Uwase who was on of the contestants for the Miss Rwanda 2019 in which she was crowned Miss Photogenic of the year.[1][2][3]
Icyo gihe yegukanye ikamba rya Nyampinga uberwa n’amafoto..[1][2][3]

The Ngarama rock or Ngarama Breast is located in Ngarama sector, Gatsibo district, Eastern province.
Urutare cyangwa se ibare rya Ngarama riherereye mu murenge wa Ngarama, Akarere ka Gatsibo,mu ntara y’iburasirazuba.
It is about twenty (20) kilometers from the place called Ibyapa bya Ngarama on Kigali-Nyagatare road.

The Rock
Urutare
This Rock is about 3 Hectares, It is very historical, as you can see the footprint of King Ruganzu II Ndoli, the well of his dogs, and even a place in the rock that sounds like a drum when you hit a stone.
Uru rutare rukabakaba hegitari eshatu, rubitse amateka cyane dore ko uhabona Ikirenge cy’Umwami Ruganzu II Ndoli,iriba ry’imbwa ze ndetse n’ingoma iri mu rutare uhondaho ibuye bikavuga nk’ingoma.
It truly shows the wisdom and greatness of that king as recorded in history.[1].
Bigaragaza koko ubwenge n’ubuhangange bw’uwo mwami nkuko bivugwa mu mateka[2].
When you stand on that rock, you can see the hills of Kimbugu, Kigasha, Kibare and Mpangare of Gitinda, as well as the Gatsibo mountain, which is named after the Gatsibo District.
Iyo uhagaze kuri urwo rutare uba witegeye udusozi twa Kimbugu,Kigasha,Kibare na Mpangare ya Gitinda, ndetse n’umusozi wa Gatsibo k’imitoma witiriwe Akarere ka Gatsibo.
History says that King Ruganzu II Ndoli was crowned from Karagwe at the home of Karemera, the local chief, where he had taken refuge.
Mu mateka havugwa nkaho umwami Ruganzu II Ndoli yimikiwe avuye Karagwe kwa Karemera uwari umutware waho aho yari yarahungiye.
