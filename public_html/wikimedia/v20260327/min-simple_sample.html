TikTok adolah layanan media sosial yang barisi vidio singkek. Di siko awak bisa mambuek vidio, mulai dari 3 detik sampai 10 minik.
TikTok is an online video sharing social networking service.

Topik vidionyo pun bisa macam-macam, mulai dari olahraga, pendidikan, pariwisata, sampai kaba berita.
It lets people create short videos that can range from 3 seconds to 10 minutes.
Awalnyo layanan ko diagiah namo Musical.ly, kudian diubah namonyo jadi TikTok.
TikTok videos can be about any topic.
Layanan ko sangaik populer dan pernah manjadi aplikasi paliang banyak diunduh di dunia pado Agustus 2023[1]. Pado taun 2024, Indonesia manjadi nagara yang paliang banyak mamakai TikTok[2].
TikTok is popular all around the world.[2] It was first released under the name of Musical.ly but was later bought out and renamed to TikTok.

Pado 3 Juli 2018, TikTok diblokir samantaro di Indonesia. Menteri Komunikasi dan Informatika, Rudiantara manyabuik, kalau di TikTok banyak konten negatif, khususnyo untuak anak-anak[1]. Manuruiknyo, pamblokiran didasari didasari dari hasil pemantau tim AIS Kominfo, pelaporan dari Kementerian Pemberdayaan Perempuan dan Anak (Kemen PPA), Komisi Perlindungan Anak Indonesia (KPAI), samo laporan dari masyarakat[2].
On 29 June 2020, India decided to ban 59 apps including TikTok.[5] A month later on 31 July 2020, US President Donald Trump announced a possible ban of the app.[6] Then, on 18 September 2020, the Trump administration announced that the app would be removed from U.S. app stores on 12 November 2020, unless ByteDance can assure the Trump administration that the app does not pose a threat to U.S. security.[7]
Pihak TikTok langsuang menanggapi pamblokiran yang dilakukan. Pihak TikTok bajanji marekrut 20 staf untuk manyensor konten TikTok di Indonesia[1]. Kominfo mancabuik pamblokiran TikTok 8 hari kudian[2].
In April 2024, President Biden signed a law that could result in the banning of TikTok in the United States unless its Chinese parent company, ByteDance, sells the app to a non-Chinese entity by January 2025.
