Ilse Johanna Christiansen (* 13 January 1954 in Fahretoft, Germany died on the 15th of may 2024) is a Northfrisian politician and activist for the north frisian people.
Ilse Johanna Christiansen (* 13. Ismuun 1954 ön Foortuft, † 15. Mai (Krölenmuun) 2024) es en nuurđfriisk Komunaalpolitikerin an aktiif ön hok Foriiningen wesen.
She has been the president of the Frasche Rädj since 2013 as the first women.
Sent 2013 es jü Fuarseter fan di Friisk Reer wesen.
For the party SSW was Ilse Christiansen active in local politics and was elected as cicvil director in Bredstedt in 2024.
Fuar di SSW es Ilse Christiansen ön di Stat Bräist en ön di Krais Nuurđfriislön aktiif wesen.
