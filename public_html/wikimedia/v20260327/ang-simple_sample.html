Mosab Al Khateeb ( Arabic : مصعب الخطيب ; exact transliteration: مصعب الخطيب), arabísk-jórdanska singer og tónskáld.
Mosab Al Khateeb ( Arabic : مصعب الخطيب ; exact transliteration: Mosab Al Khateeb), Arab-Jordanian singer and composer.

[1] Hann kom fyrst fram fyrir arabíska áhorfendur í lok árs 2013 þegar hann var hleypt af stokkunum í Star Academy dagskránni.
[1] He first appeared before the Arab audience at the end of 2013 when he was launched in the Star Academy program.
