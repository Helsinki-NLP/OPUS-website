Federal Kọleji nke Mmụta Alvan Ikoku dị na Owerri, isi obodo Imo Steeti, Naijiria. E hiwere ya n'April, 1963 dị ka kọleji ọzụzụ ndị nkuzi dị elu site n'aka gọọmentị ọwụwa anyanwụ ọwụwa anyanwụ nke Nigeria na ntọala ogige Old Shell, Owerri.[1] Kemgbe ahụ ọ gbasaa gafee okporo ụzọ Orlu dị na Osimiri Nworie. Ọ na-enye asambodo Mmụta Mba na Mmụta (NCE) na Diploma Professional in Education (PDE) yana, na mmekọ, ya na of Mahadum Nigeria, Nsukka Bachelor of Education (B.ed) kemgbe 1984.
