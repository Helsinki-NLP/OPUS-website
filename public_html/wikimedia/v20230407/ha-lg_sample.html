Frida Kahlo. Frida Kahlo (Magdalena Frida Carmen Kahlo Calderón) ye musiizi w'ebifaananyi.

Kahlo an haife shi a Coyoacán (yanzu Mexico) a shekara ta 1907, ya mutu a Coyoacán a shekara ta 1954.
Kahlo yazaalibwa mu Coyoacán (kati Mexico) mu 1907, era yafiira mu Coyoacán mu 1954.
