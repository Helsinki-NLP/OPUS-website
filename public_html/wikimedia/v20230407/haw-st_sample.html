Ua hānau ʻia ʻo ia a hānai ʻia ma Malahleni, hoʻomaka i ke kula ma Khayalethu Intermediate School a hoʻopau i kāna matric ma ke kula kiʻekiʻe o Kopanang.Ua aʻo ʻo ia i ka hana kiʻiʻoniʻoni ma ke kula ʻo Big Fish School of Digital Filmmaking ma Johannesburg a me Cape Town.
O hlahetse Malahleni a ba a holela teng, a kena sekolo sa Khayalethu Intermediate School mme a qeta lengolo la hae la materiki sekolong se phahameng sa Kopanang.O ithutetse ho etsa filimi Sekolong sa Big Fish of Digital Filmmaking Johannesburg le Cape Town.

Ua hoʻomaka ʻo ia ma ke ʻano he lālā o ka Bambanani Theatre ma 2001 a ua holomua ʻo ia i ke kūlana haku mele a me ka moʻolelo a hiki i 2004.Ma hope o ka hemo ʻana o Bambanani, ua hoʻokumu ʻo ia i kāna hui ponoʻī i kapa ʻia ʻo Spemanman Mabena's Production Theatre e kālele ana i ka hana kiʻiʻoniʻoni, ʻo Siyathuthuka Theatre. Hoʻokumu ʻia ka hana keaka me 3 Masters, kahi hui hōʻike karate a me nā mea a pau i hana ʻia ma 2005.
O qalile e le setho sa Bambanani Theatre ka 2001 mme a tsoela pele ho ba moqapi le rahistori ho fihlela 2004.Kamora hore Bambanani a tsamaee, o ile a theha k'hamphani ea hae, Spemanman Mabena's Production Theatre, e sebetsanang le ho etsa lifilimi, Siyathuthuka Theatre.Lenaneo le thehiloe ho 3 Masters, pontšo ea karate le tsohle tse etsahetseng ka 2005.
