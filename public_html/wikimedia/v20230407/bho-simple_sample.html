मिशन ज़िन्दगी, (अंग्रेजी: Mission Zindagi) भारत क गैर-लाभकारी संसथान हवे। ये संसथान मुफ्त में शिक्षा उपलब्ध करावेला।.[1][2]
Mission Zindagi is a non-profit organization based out of New Delhi. The organisation works primarily on education, women empowerment and health.[1][2]
एकर स्थापना 2017 में करल गईल।.[3]
In 2017, the organisation was formed to work in the welfare of education.[3]
मिशन ज़िन्दगी शिक्षा और स्वास्थ्य वास्ते काम करल।[4][5][6]
Mission Zindagi was founded with the objective of working in the field of education. Organisation launched a program to help students deal with the mental stress.[4][5][6]
