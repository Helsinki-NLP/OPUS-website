ᐅᓂᒃᑳᖅᑐᐊᖅ ᓄᑕᕋᑯᓗᖕᓂᙶᖅᑐᖅ
Ultraman Kids IMizekeliso Ibali (Ultraman Kids' IMizekeliso Monogatari) iya kuqhuba ukususela ngoAprili 16, 1986 ukuya kuNovemba 19, 1986, rhoqo ngooLwezithathu ukusuka 19:20 ukuya 19:30 kuthotho TBS (ngoko Fuji ) YiTV oopopayi baphathwa njengenkqubo encinci esasazwa kumabonakude iYamaguchi , eyayisisikhululo esinothotho lweTV . Zonke iziqendu ze-26 zaveliswa, kodwa iziqendu ezimbini zokugqibela (iziqendu ze-25 kunye ne-26) azizange zikhutshwe . Inikwe yinkampani enye kaMnumzana Donut .
