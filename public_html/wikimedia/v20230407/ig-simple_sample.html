Patty Aubrey bụ onye odee akwukwo si California .
Patty Aubery is an American author from California.

Ọ kwadoro usoro chicken soup maka mkpụrụ obi, gụnyere ofe ọkụkọ maka mkpụrụ obi Ndị Kraịst. [1]
She coauthored the Chicken Soup for the Soul series, including Chicken Soup for the Christian Soul.[1]
Onye mmeri nke inye ụmụ nwanyị ike, Aubrey kwadoro akwụkwọ ezubere maka naanị ụmụ nwanyị, ofe ọkụkọ maka mkpụrụ obi nwanyị nke Ndị Kraịst.
A champion of women's empowerment, Aubery co-authored a book aimed at women only, Chicken Soup for the Christian Woman's Soul.
Akwụkwọ ahụ nwere akụkọ mere eme gbasara ụmụ nwanyị na-eche ihe ịma aka ihu, oge ihe isi ike na ime ka mmadụ nwekwuo okwukwe.
The book featured true stories of women facing challenge, difficult times and renewing one's faith.
Isiakwụkwọ ndị ahụ gụnyere Okwukwe, Ịhụ Ezinụlọ n’anya, Ike Ngwọta Chineke, ọbụbụenyi, Ime mgbanwe, Ihe ịma aka na Ọrụ ebube. [1]
The chapters include Faith, The Love of Family, God's Healing Power, Friendship, Making a Difference, Challenges and Miracles.[2]
