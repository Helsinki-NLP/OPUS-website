Rishi Sunak (ruggit er 12 Boaldyn 1980),[1] she politickeyr Goaldagh va reiht myr Ard-vinishter y Reeriaght Unnaneysit as Toshiagh y Phartee Cumaadagh ayns Jerrey Fouyir 2022.
Rishi Sunak (born on the 12t Mey 1980),[1] is a British politicker wha is Prime Meenister o the Unitit Kinrick as Consertivative Pairty heid ane frae October 2022.

She Shirveishagh Tashtee yn Reeriaght Unnaneysit v'eh voish 2020 derrey 2022 as Ard-scrudeyr y Tashtee voish 2019 derrey 2020.[2] She oltey yn Pharlamaid (MP) son Richmond (Yorks) t'eh voish 2015.
He wis Chancellor o the Unitit Kinrick frae 2020 till 2022 an Chief Secretary to the Treasury frae 2019 till 2020.[2] He is Memmer o Pairlament (MP) fur Richmond (Yorks) frae 2015.
