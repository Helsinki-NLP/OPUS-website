Na Salad Juyuushi Tomatoman (Salad Jyu Yuushi Tomatoman) e dua na isoqoni ni retioyaloyalo ni anime ka dau vakayagataki ena REtioyaloyalo mai na ika 3 ni Epereli, 1992 ki na ika 27 ni Maji, 1993. TV Tokyo, TXN Kyushu (TVQ. Tabaka vata na TVQ Kyushu Broadcasting, Yomiuri Advertising Company, kei na Gakken.

Na iTukutuku ni Huckleberry Finn (いックルいリー・フィㄇいい Huckleberry Finn Monogatari?, di translated "Stories of Huckleberry Finn") e dua na anime na gaca tabaki ena 1991 by Enoki Films with 26 episodes ka yavutaki ena novel "The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn" mai ia papa Amerika Mark Twain. A tekivu vakayagataki e Japani ena NHK ena ika 26 ni Okosita, 1994 me yacova na ika 3 ni Maji, 1995. A tekivutaki mai Potukali ena 1995 ena RTP2 ena Potukali dub.
