Bramberģe was a village in Latvia, in Jelgava region, Glūda municipality next to country highway V1059.
Bramberģe bija ciems Latvijā, Jelgavas novada Glūdas pagastā pie autoceļa V1059.

Village had founded in 17.th century next to manor house of Bramberģe.
Izveidojies pie 17. gadsimtā celtās Bramberģes muižas.
After terithorial reform in 2011. Bramberģe lost it's village statuss.
Pēc 2011.gada novadu teritoriālās reformas Bramberģe zudējusi ciema statusu.
Young Gypsy Woman is a painting by Kārlis Hūns from 1870.[1] It is located in the Latvian National Museum of Art.
"Jaunā čigāniete" ir 1870. gadā gleznota Kārļa Hūna glezna.[1] Tā atrodas Latvijas Nacionālā mākslas muzeja kolekcijā.
The painting was created in the summer of 1870, when the Hūns participated in the Paris salon, and afterwards went to Normandy and then to Belgium, where this painting was created.[2]
Glezna tapusi 1870. gada vasarā, kad Hūns pēc dalības Parīzes Salonā devās uz Normandiju un pēc tam uz Beļģiju, kur šī glezna varētu būt tapusi.[2]
The Gauja Valley (Latvian Gaujas leja) is a painting by Latvian painter Jūlijs Feders from 1891 .[1][2] It is located in the Latvian National Museum of Art.
"Gaujas leja" ir 1891. gadā gleznota Jūlija Federa glezna.[1][2] Tā atrodas Latvijas Nacionālā mākslas muzeja kolekcijā.
Gaujas leja is one of the best known works of the artist's works; the painting became one of the symbols of Latvian landscape.[1]
"Gaujas leja" ir viens no Federa zināmākajiem darbiem, mākslinieka "zīmols", glezna kļuvusi par vienu no Latvijas ainavas simboliem.[1]
The painting was first exhibited at the Art Academy exhibition in St. Petersburg, after twenty years was owned by a private collector.
Glezna pirmoreiz eksponēta Mākslas akadēmijas izstādē Sanktpēterburgā, pēc tam divdesmit gadus piederējusi kādam privātkolekcionāram.
In 1921, the Riga City Art Museum purchased it.[1]
1921. gadā Rīgas Pilsētas mākslas muzejs izmantojis piedāvājumu to iegādāties.[1]

Vija Vētra (born in 1923)[2] is a Latvian dancer and choreographer.
Vija Vētra (dzimusi 1923. gada 6. februārī Rīgā)[2] ir latviešu dejotāja un horeogrāfe.
She is a leading classical Indian dancer in Latvia.[1]
Viņa ir indiešu un sakrālās dejas kustības aizsācēja Latvijā.[1]
During World War II, she studied at the Vienna Academy of Music and Fine Arts, as well as the Vienna Conservatory Ballet chapter. Wild Detroit Gang is a Latvian hard rock and rock'n'roll band from Latvia. It's a band made up of 4 different, experienced artists united by a common passion for blues rock, hard rock and rock'n'roll in general.
Wild Detroit Gang ir Latvijas smagā roka grupa, kas apvieno personības ar dažādiem raksturiem un mūzikas uztveri.
The band was formed in 29 May 2014 by Jānis Drēģeris and Gunārs Cauņa. The name of the band was inspired by the timeless Kiss band classic "Detroit Rock City".
Grupas pirmais koncerts notika 2014. gada 29. martā Latgalē, Daugavpils dzīvās mūzikas klubā “Artilērijas Pagrabi”.
The history behind it is rather simple: In the golden seventies, Kiss band cooked up the song. The song became a popular fan favorite.
Šis datums uzskatāms par grupas Wild Detroit Gang oficiālo darbības sākumu.
So popular, that the fans (and the rest of the world, eventually) deemed Detroit "The Rock City". The song, it's popularity and it's catchy name are the reasons why the band's name includes the word "Detroit". "Wild", on the other hand, tells you only one thing - the concerts will be mad!!!!
Grupas nosaukumu veido kādreiz populārās dziesmas Detroit Rock City (Kiss) atslēgvārds “Detroit”, jo 70-80 gados sabiedrību pāršalca šīs dziesmas ietekme un Detroitu sāka dēvēt arī par rokmūzikas centru.
And, finally, "Gang" signifies the gang that the band basically is - a gang of four different individuals united by a common passion and friendship. The band's primary influences are such world famous bands as AC / DC, Led Zeppelin, Deep Purple, Pink Floyd, ZZ Top, Metallica, etc. Over the short time of existence, the band has managed to gain some attention and serious contacts in the Latvian realm of rock'n'roll, most notably the legendary Latvian rock guitarist Ainars Virga.
“Wild” izsaka brīvības nozīmi grupā, “Gang” ir komandas apzīmējums.
And the band is full of determination to go further. The main idea of the band is as follows - "Music should sound from the heart. It does not matter if the heart is of iron ..."
Grupas dibinātāji Jānis Drēģeris un Gunārs Cauņa ir no Krāslavas.
In 2014 Jānis Drēģeris and Gunārs Cauņawas founded the band Wild Detroit Gang.
Grupu Wild Detroit Gang Jānis Drēģeris un Gunārs Cauņa dibināja 2014. gadā.
At this time, the band was made up of Jānis Drēģeris, Gunārs Cauņa, Tatjana Nizjuļko and Matīss Uškāns.
Toreizējo grupas sastāvu veidoja Jānis Drēģeris, Gunārs Cauņa, Tatjana Nizjuļko un Matīss Uškāns.
Initially playing only covers by ZZ Top, Deep Purple, Gary Moore, Līvi, Red Hot Chili Peppers and the like.
Sākotnēji grupa paredzēja izpildīt tikai pasaulslaveno grupu hītus.
Uncontent with playing just covers, the band set out to play their own original music. Has been written and recorder first single "Real Love Blues".
Taču neskatoties uz to, Jānis uzrakstīja pirmo singlu Real Love Blues, kurš bija atzīmējams kā oriģināldziesmu sākums.

Year 2016 was a significant year for the band due to the successful cooperation between band, guitarist Ainars Virga of the Latvian legendary band "Līvi" and Kraslava City Council. Wild Detroit Gang released a live DVD performed with Ainars Virga.
2016. gads grupai bija nozīmīgs ar to, ka veiksmīgi sadarbojoties ar Latvijas leģendārās grupas "Līvi" ģitāristu Ainaru Virgu un Krāslavas novada domi, tika realizēts projekts kopējā koncerta iemūžināšanai DVD.
