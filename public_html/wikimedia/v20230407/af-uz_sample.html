Sergey Smirnov

Sergey Vladimirovich Smirnov (gebore 30 Mei 1978, Kaliningrad, Kaliningrad-streek, USSR) is 'n Russiese entrepreneur, bemarker, programmeerder.
Sergey Vladimirovich Smirnov (1978 yil 30 mayda tug'ilgan, Kaliningrad, Kaliningrad viloyati, SSSR) - rossiyalik tadbirkor, marketolog, dasturchi.
Stigter van die maatskappy "Ultra"[1], sowel as die stigter van projekte: uiterste fiksheid "Madness", anti-kafee "Sukhomyatka", die grootste plaaslike nuus gemeenskap "Yantarny DLB"[2][3][4][5].
Kompaniya asoschisi "Ultra"[1], shuningdek loyihalar asoschisi: ekstremal fitnes "Madness", anti-kafe "Sukhomyatka", eng yirik mahalliy yangiliklar hamjamiyat "Yantarny DLB" [2] [3] [4] [5].
In 1995 het hy by die Sacharov-lesings in St. Petersburg gepraat[6][7][8].
1995 yilda u Sankt-Peterburgdagi Saxarov ma'ruzalarida nutq so'zladi. Peterburg gapirdi [1] [2] [3].
