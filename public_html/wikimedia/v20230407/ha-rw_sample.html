NDIZERA Ange ɗan ƙasar Ruwanda ne kuma kwararren ɗan jaridan wasanni ne.
NDIZERA Ange ni umunyarwanda akaba umunyamakuru wa siporo ni mikino wa bigize umwuga.

NDIZERA Ange ya yi aiki a kamfanonin watsa labaru daban-daban kamar yadda ya fara a Energy Radio FM, ya ci gaba da aiki a matsayin dan jarida na wasanni a wani kamfanin watsa labarai na wasanni a Isango Star da Isango TV Rwanda, inda yake ba da labaran wasanni da wasanni.
NDIZERA Ange yakoze kigo by'ibitangazamakuru bitandukanye nkaho yatangiriye kuri Energy Radio FM, yakomeje akazi k’itangazamakuru rya siporo ku kindi kigo cy'itangazamakuru cya siporo kuri Isango Star hamwe na Isango TV Rwanda, aha hose yavugaga imikino n’inkuru za siporo.
NDIZERA Ange memba ne na kungiyar 'yan jarida ta wasanni AJSPOR NDIZERA Ange masoyin Manchester United ne daga Ingila a nahiyar Turai. [1] [2] [3] [4] [5]
NDIZERA Ange abarizwa mu ishyirahamwe rya banyamakuru ba siporo AJSPOR NDIZERA Ange ni umufana wa Manchester United yo mu gihugu cyu bwongereza ku mugabane w'iburayi.[1][2][3][4][5]

Abraham Niyonkuru (an haife shi a ranar 26 ga watan Disamba 1989) ɗan wasan guje-guje da tsalle-tsalle ne ɗan ƙasar Burundi wanda ya ƙware a tseren gudun fanfalaki, mita 10,000 da guje-guje.
Niyonkuru Abraham (wavutse ku ya 26 Ukuboza 1989) ni umukinnyi wo gusiganwa ku maguru mu Burundi wiruka muri marato ya metero 10,000.
Niyonkuru ya fafata a gasar Olympics ta bazara ta 2016 a Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
Niyonkuru yitabiriye imikino y'Olempike yo mu 2016 yabereye i Rio de Janeiro, muri Burezili.
A gasar Olympics, ya yi gasar tseren gudun fanfalaki.
Mu mikino Olempike, .
Niyonkuru kuma ya fafata a Gasar Kananan Hukumomin Duniya, Gasar Cin Kofin Ƙasa ta Duniya guda biyu, Jeux de la Francophonie, Gasar world military t ta Duniya da Gasar track and field da Auray-Vannes Half Marathon.
Niyonkuru yanitabiriye amarushanwa ya Shampiyona y’abato ku isi, Amarushanwa abiri y’isi, Jeux de la Francophonie, Shampiyona y’abasirikare ku isi, Shampiyona y’imikino ngororamubiri na Marato ya Auray-Vannes.[1]
Farkon wasan Niyonkuru a gasar kasa da kasa shine a Gasar Cin Kofin Kasashen Duniya ta IAAF ta 2005.
Umukino wa mbere wa Niyonkuru yari muri Shampiyona y'isi ya IAAF 2005.
Ya yi takara a tseren yara kanana kuma ya kare a na 33 a cikin ’yan wasa 133 [lower-alpha 1] a cikin mintuna 25 da dakika 32.[2] [1] A 2005 Jeux de la Francophonie Niyonkuru ya fafata a tseren mita 10,000.
Yarushanwe mu marushanwa y'abato yarangije ku mwanya wa 33 mu bakinnyi 133 [lower-alpha 1] akoresheje iminota 25 n'amasegonda 32. [1] Muri 2005 Jeux de la Francophonie Niyonkuru yarushanwe muri metero 10,000.
[3] Niyonkuru ya zo na hudu a cikin mintuna 29 da dakika 18.08, dakika 0.03 kacal a bayan wanda ya samu lambar azurfa, dan kasar Morocco Abderrahim Goumri.
[2] Niyonkuru yarangije ku mwanya wa kane mu minota 29 n'amasegonda 18.08, amasegonda 0.03 nu mudari wa silver, Maroc Abderrahim Goumri.
[4] Daga nan Niyonkuru ya fafata a gasar matasa ta duniya a shekara ta shekarar 2006.[5] Ya zo na biyar a tseren mita 10,000 a cikin mintuna 28 da dakika 59.92.
Niyonkuru yahise yitabira Shampiyona y'urubyiruko ku isi mu 2006. [3] Yarangije ku mwanya wa gatanu muri metero 10,000 akoresheje iminota 28 n'amasegonda 59.92.
[6] Ya yi dakika 6.63 a bayan wanda ya lashe tseren, Ibrahim Jeilan na Habasha.
Yasubiye inyuma ho amasegonda inyuma inyuma yuwatsinze iri siganwa, Ibrahim Jeilan wo muri Etiyopiya.
[6] A shekara ta 2007, Niyonkuru ya fafata a gasar cin kofin duniya ta shekarar 2007 a gasar tseren kananan yara.[7] Ya zo na 13 a cikin mintuna 24 da dakika 56.
Mu 2007, Niyonkuru yitabiriye igikombe cy'isi cy'abato 2007. [4] Yarangije ku mwanya wa 13 mu minota 24 n'amasegonda 56.
[8] Ya kammala dakika 49 a bayan wanda ya lashe lambar zinare, dan kasar Kenya Asbel Kiprop.
Yarangije amasegonda 49 inyuma yuwabonye umudali wa zahabu, Umunyakenya Asbel Kiprop.
[8] A Gasar Wasan Soja ta Duniya ta 2009, Niyonkuru ya lashe lambar azurfa a tseren mita 10,000.[9] Tsawon mintuna 29 da dakika 37.14 ya kasance dakika 23.67 bayan dan wasan da ya lashe kyautar zinare, Essa Ismail Rashed na Qatar.
Mu mikino ya gisirikare yisi ya 2009, Niyonkuru yegukanye umudari wa feza muri metero 10,000. [5] Igihe cye cy'iminota 29 n'amasegonda 37.14 cyari amasegonda 23.67 inyuma yuwabonye yumudari wa zahabu, Essa Ismail Rashed wo muri Qatar.
[10] Niyonkuru ya wuce dakika 4.97 a gaban wanda ya samu lambar tagulla, dan kasar Tunisiya El Akhdar Hachani.
Niyonkuru yarushije amasegonda 4.97 imbere yumudari wa bronze, Tuniziya El Akhdar Hachani .
[10] Niyonkuru ya lashe tseren rabin Marathon na Auray-Vannes na 2015 a cikin sa'a daya da minti uku da dakika ashirin.[11] A gasar Olympics ta bazara ta 2016, Niyonkuru ya fafata a tseren gudun fanfalaki na maza a ranar 21 ga watan Agustan 2016, amma bai gama gasar ba.
Niyonkuru yatsinze igice cya Marato ya Auray-Vannes 2015 mu isaha imwe iminota itatu amasegonda makumyabiri. [6] Mu mikino Olempike yo mu 2016, Niyonkuru yitabiriye marato y'abagabo ku ya 21 Kanama 2016, ariko ntiyarangije urushanwa.
[12] A cikin shekarar 2017 ya yi takara a tseren gudun fanfalaki na maza a Gasar Cin Kofin Duniya na 2017, inda ya sanya na 71 a cikin 2:42:27.[13]
[7] Muri 2017 yitabiriye marato y'abagabo muri Shampiyona y'isi, ashyira ku mwanya wa 71 akoresheje 2:42:27. [8]

Francine Niyonizigiye (an haife ta a ranar 26 ga watan Satumba 1988)[1] 'yar wasan Burundi ce wacce ta kware a tseren nesa.[2]
Niyonizigiye Francine (wavutse ku ya 26 Nzeri 1988) [1] ni umukinnyi wu mu Burundi uzobereye mu mukino wo kwiruka . [2]
Ta shiga gasar Olympics ta lokacin zafi da aka yi a birnin Beijing na shekarar 2008, a tseren mita 5000, inda ta zo ta goma sha hudu a cikin zafi a cikin 17:08.44.
Yitabiriye imikino y'Olempike yo mu 2008 yabereye i Beijingmu kwiruka metero 5000, arangiza ku mwanya wa 14 akoresheje imitona 17: 08.44.
Niyonizigiye ita ma ta kasance mai rike da tuta a wajen bude taron.[3]
Niyonizigiye niwe wari afite ibendera ryigihugu mu birori byo gutangiza amarushanwa. [1]
Vénuste Niyongabo OLY [1] (an haife shi a watan Disamba 9, 1973) tsohon ɗan wasan tsere ne mai tsayi da tsaka-tsaki.
Niyongabo Vénuste OLY [1] (yavutse ku ya 9 Ukuboza 1973) yahoze yirukanka muri marato .
A cikin shekarar 1996, ya zama dan wasa na farko da ya samu lambar yabo ta Olympics daga Burundi ta hanyar lashe tseren mita 5000 a gasar Olympics ta bazara ta 1996. [2] Ya taba yin takara sau biyu a baya a wannan taron kafin ya lashe lambar zinare.
Mu 1996, abaye umukinnyi wa mbere ukomoka mu Burundi wegukanye umudari wa Olempike metero 5000 mu mikino Olempike yo mu 1996 [2] yarushanwe na none mu ma rushanwa umudari wa zahabu.[3]

IAAF.
An haife shi a Vugizo, a kudancin Burundi, [3] Niyongabo ya ci lambar azurfa a cikin 1500 mita a gasar matasa ta duniya na shekarar 1992 kuma ya zo na hudu sama da mita 800.
Niyongabo yavukiye i Vugizo, mu majyepfo y’Uburundi, [1] Niyongabo yegukanye umudari wa feza muri metero 1500 muri Shampiyona y’isi y’urubyiruko, mu 1992 yaje mwanya wa kane muri metero 800.
Ya yi takara a babban gasarsa ta farko a shekara mai zuwa, amma an cire shi a wasan kusa da na karshe na 1500 m na gasar cin kofin duniya a Stuttgart.
Yitabiriye amarushanwa ya mbere akomeye mu mwaka wakurikiyeho, ariko asezererwa muri kimwe cya kabiri kirangiza mu gikombe cyisi cya metero 1500 cyabereye i Stuttgart .
Ba da daɗewa ba Niyongabo ya zama ɗaya daga cikin masu tseren mita 1500 a duniya, inda ya lashe manyan gasa da dama a 1994 da 1995.
Niyongabo yahise aba umwe mu basiganwa ku maguru metero 1500 ku isi, yegukana imidali byinshi muri shampiyona mu 1994 na 1995.
Ya kuma lashe lambar tagulla a gasar cin kofin duniya da aka yi a Gothenburg inda ya kare a bayan Noureddine Morceli da Hicham El Guerrouj. [2]
Yatsindiye kandi umudari wa bronze mu gikombe cy'isi cyabereye i Gothenburg aho yarangirije inyuma ya Noureddine Morceli na Hicham El Guerrouj .[2]
Ga wasannin Olympics na 1996, da aka gudanar a Atlanta, an ɗauka Niyongabo zai zama wanda zai iya lashe gasar 1500. m, amma ya yanke shawarar barin wurinsa ga wani dan kasar, Dieudonné Kwizera.
Mu mikino Olempike yo mu 1996, yabereye i Atlanta, Niyongabo yafatwaga nk'uwashobora gutsinda isiganwa 1500 m, ariko yahisemo kuva mu mwanya awurekera mugenziwe, Dieudonné Kwizera.
Kwizera ya kasa shiga gasar Olympics a 1988 da 1992 tun lokacin da Burundi ba ta da kwamitin Olympics na kasa a lokacin, kuma yana Atlanta ne kawai a matsayin koci.
Kwizera yananiwe kwitabira imikino Olempike mu 1988 na 1992 kubera ko icyo gihe u Burundi avukamo butari bufite komite y'igihugu y'imikino y'Olempike, kandi yari i Atlanta gusa nk'umutoza.
Niyongabo ya hau zuwa gasa a cikin 5000 m taron maimakon.
Niyongabo yagiye kurushanwa muri metero 5000.
