Ionuț Silaghi de Oaș, Count de Szilágyi-Oaș[1] (born March 16, 1990) is a Romanian singer, songwriter,[2][3][4][5][6] producer,[7] collector of folk music, author, aristocrat, entrepreneur[8] and engineer.[9] He is the current head of the Szilágyi-Oaș noble family and the current Secretary General of Ansamblul Oaşul.[10][11][12] He is a graduate of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca and of the Babeş-Bolyai University.

Ang Beganismo (iningles: Veganism) mao usa ka pilosopiya nga nag-ingon nga ang mga tawo dili kinahanglan mogamit og mga hayop.
Veganism is a philosophy that says people should not use animals.
Adunay mga vegan nga nga dili mokaon sa mga hayop, ug ang mga vegan nga nagpili nga dili usab gamiton kini sa bisan unsang paagi.
There are vegans who choose not to eat animals, and vegans who choose not to use them in any other way either.
Ang mga Vegan dili mokaon o moinom og pagkaon nga gikan sa mga hayop, lakip ang karne, itlog ug mga produkto sa dairy (sama sa gatas, keso, ug yogurt).
Vegans do not eat or drink food that comes from animals, including meat, eggs and dairy products (like milk, cheese, and yogurt). A vegan diet is sometimes called a strict vegetarian diet. Some vegans also do not eat honey.[1] Many vegans try not to use any other animal products, such as leather, wool, feathers, bone, or pearl.
