Jizzakh branch of the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek - Higher education institution located in Jizzakh city, Jizzakh region.
Mirzo Ulugʻbek nomidagi Oʻzbekiston milliy universitetining Jizzax filiali — Oʻzbekiston Respublikasi, Jizzax viloyati, Jizzax shahrida joylashgan oliy taʼlim muassasasi.
Established in 2019.
2019-yilda tashkil topgan.
At the same time, Shermuhammedova Nigina Aslanovna Professor of the Department of Philosophy and Logic at the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek, Doctor of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, Professor H. Hasanov Head of the Department of "Differential Equations" at Samarkand State University are also hired in accordance with the established procedure
Shu bilan bir qatorda Mirzo Ulugʻbek nomidagi Oʻzbekiston Milliy universiteti „Falsafa va mantiq“ kafedrasi professori Shermuhammedova Nigina Aslanovna, Samarqand Davlat universiteti „Differensial tenglamalar“ kafedrasi mudiri fizika-matematika fanlari doktori, professor H.Hasanovlar ham belgilangan tartib asosida ishga qabul qilindi.
The branch is planning to introduce modern educational technologies and methods of distance learning in the educational process with the involvement of professors and teachers from leading foreign universities.
Filialda xorijiy davlatlarning etakchi oliy taʼlim muassasalari professor-oʻqituvchilarini jalb etish orqali oʻquv jarayoniga zamonaviy taʼlim texnologiyalarini va masofaviy oʻqitish usullarini joriy etish rejalashtirilgan.
The admission plans for the 2019-2020 academic year in partnership with the leading foreign universities, including Kazan Federal University (3+1) for the training of leading specialists in psychology, the acceptance rate was 25 out of 87 students, who submitted applications and took the entrance exams.
Xorijiy davlatlarning etakchi oliy taʼlim muassasalari bilan, jumladan, psixologiya sohasi boʻyicha etakchi kadrlar tayyorlash maqsadida Rossiya Federatsiyasining Qozon federal universiteti bilan hamkorlikda (3+1) qoʻshma taʼlimi dasturi doirasida 2019-2020 oʻquv yili uchun qabul rejasi 25 nafar etib belgilandi va 87 nafar abiturientlar hujjat va kirish imtixonlarini topshirdi.
An agreement was signed between the branch and Kazan Federal University on the issue of dual diplomas and training.
Filial va Rossiya Federatsiyasining Qozon federal universiteti oʻrtasida ikki tomonlama diplom berish hamda oʻquv jarayonlarini tashkil etish boʻyicha shartnoma imzolandi.
In cooperation with the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek the branch attracts highly qualified professors and teachers, including specialists with extensive practical experience in psychology.
Mirzo Ulugʻbek nomidagi Oʻzbekiston Milliy universiteti bilan hamkorlikda filialga tanlov asosida, shu jumladan, psixologiya sohasida katta amaliy tajribaga ega boʻlgan mutaxassislardan iborat yuqori malakali professor-oʻqituvchilar jalb qilinmoqda.
It is planned as a promising task to establish partnerships with leading universities and research institutions of the Russian Federation to carry out scientific-practical research aimed at solving the scientific tasks of psychology.
Rossiya Federatsiyasining etakchi oliy taʼlim muassasalari va ilmiy-tadqiqot muassasalari bilan psixologiyaning ilmiy vazifalarini xal etishga yoʻnaltirilgan ilmiy-amaliy tadqiqotlar olib borish boʻyicha hamkorlik aloqalarini oʻrnatish istiqbolli vazifa sifatida rejalashtirilgan.

The Jizzakh branch of the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek was established in Jizzakh by the resolution of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan No. 563 dated July 5, 2019 and the decision of the regional governor No. 261 dated July 10, 2019.
Vazirlar Mahkamasining 2019 yil 5 iyuldagi 563-sonli qarori hamda viloyat hokimining 2019 yil 10 iyuldagi 261-sonli qarori bilan Jizzax shahrida Mirzo Ulugʻbek nomidagi Oʻzbekiston Milliy universitetining Jizzax filiali tashkil etildi.
In accordance with the decision of the State Commission for Admission to the educational institutions of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the following four educational directions for the academic year 2019/2020 has been approved for Jizzakh branch of the National University of Uzbekistan named after Mirzo Ulugbek.
2019/2020 oʻquv yilida filialda davlat byuurtmasi asosida oʻqishga qabul qilish parametrlari OʻzR taʼlim muassasalariga qabul qilish boʻyicha davlat komissiyasining qaroriga muvofiq Mirzo Ulugʻbek nomidagi Oʻzbekiston Milliy universiteti Jizzax filialining 2019-2020 oʻquv yiliga toʻrtta taʼlim yoʻnalishi boʻyicha qabul rejasi quyidagicha tasdiqlandi:
Psychology (family psychology) 50 students
Psixologiya (oila psixologiyasi) 50 nafar
Psychology (youth psychology) 25 students
Psixologiya (yoshlar psixologiyasi) 25 nafar
Applied mathematics and informatics 50 students
Amaliy matematika va informatika 50 nafar
Informatics and Information Technology 25 students
Informatika va axborot texnologiyalari 25 nafar
