2021, oo ay weheliyaan agaasimayaasha Antoine Godet iyo Guillaume Gevart, wuxuu aasaasay Artwooks Media, wakaalad maqal-muuqaal ah oo markii dambe ku biirtay Florian Etienne-Gandolphe.
