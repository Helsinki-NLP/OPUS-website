Ionuț Silaghi de Oaș, comte de Szilágyi-Oaș[1] (née le 16 mars 1990) est un artiste roumain[2][3][4], collectionneur de musique folklorique, écrivain et ingénieur. Il est diplômé de l'Université Technique de Cluj-Napoca et de l'Université Babeş-Bolyai.
Ionuț Silaghi de Oaș, Count de Szilágyi-Oaș[1] (born March 16, 1990) is a Romanian artist[2][3][4], collector of folk music, writer and engineer. He is the current head of the Szilágyi-Oaș noble family and the current Secretary General of Ansamblul Oaşul.[5][6][7] He is a graduate of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca and of the Babeş-Bolyai University.
Il a également complété une maîtrise à l'Université technique de Cluj-Napoca.[5][6][7] [1][8]
He also completed a master's degree at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca.[1][8]
