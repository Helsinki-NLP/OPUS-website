Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson (born 15 April 1990)[1] is an English actress, model and activist. Born in Paris and brought up in Oxfordshire, Watson attended the Dragon School and trained as an actress at the Oxford branch of Stagecoach Theatre Arts. As a child, she rose to prominence with her first professional acting role as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series, having acted only in school plays previously.
Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson (inianak 15 Abril 1990)[1] sakey na aktres sa Ingles, modelo san aktibista.

The English poet Christopher Smart (1722–1771) was confined to mental asylums from May 1757 until January 1763.
Say ingles a managsulat Christopher Smart (1722–1771) sikatoy nipriso ed mental asylum manlupud Mayo 1757 anggad Enero 1763.
Smart was admitted to St Luke's Hospital for Lunatics, Upper Moorfields, London, on 6 May 1757.
Si Smart so inawat ed St. Luke's Hospital for Lunatics, Upper Moorfields, London, nen 6 Mayo 1757.
He was taken there by his father-in-law, John Newbery, although he may have been confined in a private madhouse before then.
Sikatoy inawit diman na katulangan ton si John Newbery, anggaman nayarin sikatoy nipriso ed sakey a pribadon ospital parad saray buang.
While in St Luke's he wrote Jubilate Agno and A Song to David, the poems considered to be his greatest works.
Legan a walad St. Luke's et insulat to so Jubilate Agno tan A Song to David, saray anlong ya ipapasen a sankamaongan a gawa to.
Although many of his contemporaries agreed that Smart was "mad", accounts of his condition and its ramifications varied, and some felt that he had been committed unfairly.
Anggaman dakel ed saray kapanaonan to so mipakna a si Smart et "sintinsiaan", nanduruma iray salaysay nipaakar ed kipapasen to tan saray nansumpalan na satan, tan walaray maniisip a sikatoy agmatunong.
Smart was diagnosed as "incurable" while at St Luke's, and when they ran out of funds for his care he was moved to Mr. Potter's asylum, Bethnal Green.
Si Smart et "agnasarag a paabigen" legan a walad St. Luke, tan sanen naupot so pundo parad pangasikaso ed sikato et inyalis ed Mr. Say asylum nen Potter, Bethnal Green.
All that is known of his years of confinement is that he wrote poetry.
Say amta tayo labat nipaakar ed impanpriso to et sikatoy nansulat na anlong.
Smart's isolation led him to abandon the poetic genres of the 18th century that had marked his earlier work and to write religious poetry such as Jubilate Agno ("Rejoice in the Lamb").
Say inka-isolado nen Smart so amakiwas ed sikaton ikaindan iray anlong na anlong nen koma-18 a siglo a nankabaliksan na apalabas a kimey to tan mansulat na relihyoson anlong a singa say Jubilate Agno ("Manliket ed Kordero").
His asylum poetry reveals a desire for "unmediated revelation", and it is possible that the self-evaluation found in his poetry represents an expression of evangelical Christianity.
Ipapanengneng na saray anlong to a labay ton "naliktar a tampol", tan posiblin say panag-usisa ed sarili ya walad saray anlong to et pakapatnagan na ebanghelisyal a Inkakristiano.
Late 18th-century critics felt that Smart's madness justified them in ignoring his A Song to David, but during the following century Robert Browning and his contemporaries considered his condition to be the source of his genius.
Saray kritiko ed bandad sampot na koma-18 a siglo et inisip dan say inkabulok nen Smart so angiter ed sikara na rason pian ibaliwala so A Song to David to, balet diad tinmumbok a siglo et inisip nen Robert Browning tan saray kapanaon to ya say kipapasen to so lapuan na inkasilib to.
It was not until the 20th century, with the rediscovery of Jubilate Agno (not published until 1939), that critics reconsidered Smart's case and began to see him as a revolutionary poet, the possible target of a plot by his father-in-law, a publisher, to silence him.
Diad koma-20 a siglo, sanen adiskobre lamet so Jubilate Agno (agni nipalapag anggad 1939), saray kritiko et ninonot da lamet so kaso nen Smart tan sikatoy impasen dan sakey a rebolusyonaryo a managsulat, say posiblin puntirya na plano na katulangan to, sakey ya publisher, pian sikato so patundaen.

Cabanatuan, officially the City of Cabanatuan (Filipino: Lungsod ng Cabanatuan; Ilocano: Siudad ti Cabanatuan), is a 1st class component city in the province of Nueva Ecija, Philippines.
Cabanatuan, so opisyal ya lungsod ed Canabatuan (Filipino: Lungsod ya Cabanatuan; Ilocano: Siudad ti Cabanatuan), a unaan ya klase na syudad ya kabaingan ed province ya Nueva Ecija, Philippines.
According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 327,325 people, [3] making it the most populous city in Nueva Ecija and the fifth-most populous in Central Luzon.
Unong ed 2020 census, walay population ya 327,325 tuo, manggagawa ya karakel ya tuo ed lungsod na Nueva Ecija tan panlimaran marlak ya populasyon ed Central Luzon.
The city is popular for being home to more than 30,000 motorized tricycles, making it the "Tricycle Capital of the Philippines."[5] Its strategic location along the Cagayan Valley Road has made the city a major economic, educational, medical, entertainment, shopping, and transportation center in Nueva Ecija and nearby provinces in the region such as Tarlac, Aurora, and Bulacan. It has also earned the moniker "Gateway to the North."
Dahil walay masulok ya 30,000 ya rehistradong tricycle, aya so walay titulon "Tricycle Capital of the Philippines". A strategic lokasyon diad Cagayan Valley Road ginawa ya syudad ya manuna ekonomya, edukasyon, medikal, panagligliwaan, panag-shopping tan panag-transpotasyon center ed Nueva Ecija, tan asingger ya province ed region singa ed Tarlac, Aurora tan Bulacan.

Jenna Marie Ortega (inyanak nen Septyembre 27, 2002) sakey ya American aktres.
She began her career as a child actress, receiving recognition for her role as young Jane in The CW comedy-drama series Jane the Virgin (2014–2019).
Inumpisaan to so karera to bilang ugaw ya mangaganap, timanggap ya rekognasyon jad panggaganap to bilang ugaw ya Jane jad The CW komedya-drama serye.
Her breakthrough role came in portraying Harley Diaz in the Disney Channel series Stuck in the Middle (2016–2018), for which she won an Imagen Award.
Jane the Virgin (2014-2019). Mas simikat ed role to bilang Harley Diaz ja ed Disney Channel serye na Stuck in the Middle (2016-2018), na akatanggap ya Imagen Award.
She played Ellie Alves in the second season of the thriller series You in 2019 and starred in the family film Yes Day (2021), both for Netflix.
Gimanap bilang Ellie Alves jad ikadwaran season to may thriller serye na You nen 2019 insan limabas met ed family film na Yes Day (2021), parehas ya inlabas et Netflix
Ortega received critical praise for her performance in the teen drama film The Fallout (2021), and went on to star in the 2022 horror films Scream and X. Miley Ray Cyrus Pinanganak noong Novembre 23, 1992 sa Destiny Hope Cyrus.
Her music spans across varied styles and genres, including pop, country, rock, hip hop, and experimental music.
Ay isang Amerikanong mang-aawit, manunulat ng kanta, at artista.
She has attained the most (13) US Billboard 200 top-five albums in the 21st century by a female artist.
Ang kanyang musika ay sumasaklaw sa iba't ibang istilo at genre, kabilang ang pop, country, rock, hip hop, at pang-eksperimentong musika.
