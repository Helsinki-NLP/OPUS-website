Obugazi
Tshebediso
Akaserea kano tulina amatabi 6 (Buslovakia, Bucheki, Ostuleriya, Buyindi, Tayilandi, Sawusi Afilika wamu ne Tanzaniya). Tuyambibwako abantu abassuka mu 200 wamu n’abakunganya b’ensigo okuva mu nsi yona.
Ha jwale, KPR e na le makala a maholo naheng tse tsheletseng. Tsona ke Slovakia, Czechia, Australia, India, Thailand, Afrika Borwa le Tanzania. Re na le bo-radikoporasi le babokelli ba dipeo ba fetang makholo a mabedi (200).
Akasera kino tusobola okukunganya era n’okubunyisa ebika by’ebibala ebisoba mu 10,000 okuva mu nsi yona.
Morao tjena se re kgona ho bokella le ho rekisa mefuta e fetang 10 000 ya dimela tsa lefatshe lohle.
