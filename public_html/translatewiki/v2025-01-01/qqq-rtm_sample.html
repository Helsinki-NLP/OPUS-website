(src)="3006.4"> It includes the main page and pages in the main namespace and any other namespaces that are included when the wiki is customised .
(src)="3006.5"> For example on Wikimedia Commons ' content pages ' include pages in the file and category namespaces .
(src)="3006.6"> On Wikinews ' content pages ' include pages in the Portal namespace .
(src)="3006.7"> For a technical definition of ' content namespaces ' see [ [ mw : Manual : Using_custom_namespaces # Content_namespaces | MediaWiki ] ] .
(src)="3006.8">
(src)="3006.9"> Possible alternatives to the word ' content ' are ' subject matter ' or ' wiki subject ' or ' wiki purpose ' .
