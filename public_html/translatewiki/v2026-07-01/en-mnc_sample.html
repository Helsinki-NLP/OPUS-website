What is Adiutor?
Adiutor serengge ai?
Adiutor is a tool developed to make maintenance tasks on Wikipedia more efficient and user-friendly. Built using OOUI-based MediaWiki JS and JavaScript, Adiutor is designed to allow users to perform various tasks with ease. All suggestions and bug reports are reviewed and discussed on the talk page for continuous development and improvement of the tool. The active participation and support of the Wikipedia community plays an important role in making Adiutor a more functional and user-friendly tool.
Adiutor oci Uikipediya be karmara tušan -i tusangga kemun jai ja -i baitalara banin be wesihulebure be dahame araha tetun. Adiutor OOUI -i MediaWiki JS jai JavaScript be fulehe obume arahangge, ini dezain yaya baitalasi hacingga tušan be ja -i šanggabuci ombi. Yaya tucibure gisun jai tašaraha puroguram gemu leolere afaha de acabume leolere, uttu ofi nakarakū arame tetun be halame ibebuci ojoro. Uikipediya gerenusu oron -i adaname urušerengge Adiutor be elei etuhun, elei ja -i baitalame ojoro tetun obuhabi.
