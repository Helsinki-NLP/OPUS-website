Turn battery optimization off?
Gwekin ishkode-makakoons ishkon gidiskise?
Uploading more than 3 images works more reliably when the battery optimization is turned off. Please turn battery optimization off for the Commons app from the settings for a smooth upload experience. Possible steps to turn battery optimization off: Step 1: Tap on the 'Settings' button below. Step 2: Switch from 'Not optimized' to 'All apps'. Step 3: Search for "Commons" or "fr.free.nrw.commons". Step 4: Tap it and select 'Don't optimize'. Step 5: Press 'Done'.
Adim nawaj niso mazinaakizoned gashkanokii mino apii ishkode-makakoons ishkon gidiskise. Daga gwekin ishkode-makakoons ishkon gidiskise biindig oshtigwaanzhaabonigan mazinaabikiwebinigan-agaasaa'makak biindig bagida'waa biindig wendad adim. Aawi dakokii biindig gwekin ishkode-makakoons ishkon gidiskise: Dakokii bezhig: Daangin 'Bagida'waa' gibadoonh anaamayi'ii. Dakokii niizho: Aanji biindig 'gaawiin ishkon' biindig 'akina mazinaabikiwebinigan-agaasaa'makak'. Dakokii niso: Nandawaabam biindig "Oshtigwaanzhaabonigan" gemaa "fr.free.nrw.commons". Dakokii niiyo: Daangin gaye onaabam 'Gaawiin ishkon'. Dakokii naano: 'Doodan'.
