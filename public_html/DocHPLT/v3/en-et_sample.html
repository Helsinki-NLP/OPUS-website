Consumer price index continued to rise in April
Tarbijahinnaindeksi tõus jätkus
According to Statistics Estonia, the change of the consumer price index in April 2018 was 0.4% compared to March 2018 and 2.9% compared to April of the previous year.
Tarbijahinnaindeksi muutus oli 2018. aasta mais võrreldes aprilliga 0,3% ja võrreldes eelmise aasta maiga 3,0%, teatab Statistikaamet.
