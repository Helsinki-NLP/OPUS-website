Palestinian Legislative Council
Consello Lexislativo Palestino
The Palestinian Legislative Council (PLC), also referred to as the Palestinian Parliament, is a unicameral body with 132 members, elected from 16 electoral districts in the West Bank and Gaza. It is the legislature of the Palestinian Authority.
O Consello Lexislativo Palestino (ás veces referido ao Parlamento Palestino), o poder lexislativo da Autoridade Nacional Palestina, é unha unicamera con 132 membros, elixidos nos 16 distritos electorais en Cisxordania e Gaza.
The Palestinian Legislative Council passed a new law in June 2005 increasing the number of members from 88 to 132, stipulating that half be elected under a system of proportional representation and half by plurality-at-large voting in traditional constituencies.
O Consello Lexislativo Palestino aprobou unha nova lei en xuño de 2005, incrementando o número de membros de 88 a 132, dos cales a metade son elixidos mediante un sistema de Representación proporcional e a outra metade por escrutinio maioritario plurinomial.
The last parliamentary elections took place on 25 January 2006.
Tiveron lugar unhas novas eleccións o 25 de xaneiro de 2006.
The Palestinian legislative council has been unable to meet and govern since 2007 due to the Israeli imprisonment of some members, the Fatah–Hamas conflict and the indefinite postponing of elections by the Fatah leadership.[1]
O Consello Lexislativo Palestino non puido gobernar debido a que algúns dos seus membros están presos por Israel, o Conflito entre Hamás e Fatah e o aplazamento indefinido das eleccións por parte dos dirixentes de Fatah.[2][3]
The emblem used for the Palestinian Legislative Council is referred to as the "Eagle of Saladin."
O emblema utilizado polo Consello Lexislativo Palestino coñécese como "Aguia de Saladino."
