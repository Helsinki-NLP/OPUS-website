    <s id="5.4">To that end, it is essential to monitor weather conditions and adjust harvesting schedules accordingly.</s>

    <s id="6.1">When determining the optimal time to harvest honey, beekeepers should consider the growth cycle of their bees.</s>
    <s id="6.2">New colonies may not produce much honey during their first year since they are focused on building comb and storing honey to feed the colony.</s>
    <s id="6.3">Harvesting honey during the first year may leave the bees with insufficient amounts to sustain themselves, especially during the winter months.</s>
    <s id="6.4">It is advisable to wait until the colony has had sufficient time to grow in population and strength before harvesting honey.</s>
