The night after another unsatisfactory New Year party, Tim’s father (Bill Nighy) tells his son that the men in his family have always had the ability to travel through time.
Oni će se zaljubiti, ali će se desiti jedna nezgoda prilikom putovanja kroz vrijeme što će prouzrokovati to da se nikad nisu ni sreli.
They fall in love, then an unfortunate time-travel incident means he’s never met her at all.
Onda će se ponovo po prvi put upoznati… i opet… ali konačno, posle mnogo vještih putovanja kroz vrijeme, on će osvojiti njeno srce.
So they meet for the first time again—and again—but finally, after a lot of cunning time traveling, he wins her heart.
