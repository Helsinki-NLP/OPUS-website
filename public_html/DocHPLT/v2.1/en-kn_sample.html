Manjeshwar Govinda Pai (Kannada: ಮಂಜೇಶ್ವರ ಗೋವಿಂದ ಪೈ), also known as Rastrakavi Govinda Pai, was a Kannada poet.
Manjeshwar Govinda Pai (Kannada: ಮಂಜೇಶ್ವರ ಗೋವಿಂದ ಪೈ), also known as Rastrakavi Govinda Pai, was a Kannada poet.
