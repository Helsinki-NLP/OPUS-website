  • we allow uploading of a pre-defined list of data formats
  • we allow uploads of archives (zip, tar, tar.gz, tgz)
  • no sub-directories in archives! (will be converted to flat structures)
  • only ascii characters in all file names! (all other characters will be deleted!)
  • parallel documents have to have identical names!
    • one upload per language (maybe archives with MANY files!)
    • alignment will automatically be initiated if documents with identical names are found in the repository!

The upload (archives or single files) will be placed in the appropriate directory!

  • TMX: letsmt/<resource>/uploads/tmx/filename
  • XLIFF: letsmt/<resource>/uploads/xliff/filename
  • DOC: letsmt/<resource>/uploads/doc>/<lang>/filename

After uploading: preprocessing will be auto-magically be initiated by the REST-server!
