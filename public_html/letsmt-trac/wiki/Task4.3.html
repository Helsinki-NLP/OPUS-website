Task 4.3 Collection of SMT training resources (D4.3, D4.4, D4.9)

In this task project partners will (i) upload general training data already available at their disposal, and (ii) additional publicly accessible training data will also be collected (see Table B.3.2.a.1 about the amount and the nature of the training data for collecting). In parallel with the handling of already available data, the work package will (iii) collect the first (collections of) texts from text providers. The first collected data (D4.3/M12) will be used to test the SMT Resource Repository (WP2) and to provide data for the initial SMT training resources (WP3). In order to ensure a reasonable quality of the output from the SMT systems for general usage, this task handles the continued collection of texts from text providers in order to assure that the system will be populated with large amounts of SMT training data during the last year of the project (D4.4/M30, see Table B.3.2.a.2 about the amount and the nature of the training data for collecting). Collected SMT training data will be acquired and uploaded to the LetsMT! platform.

(UCPH, TILDE, UUP, FFZG, UEDIN, MOR, SEM)
