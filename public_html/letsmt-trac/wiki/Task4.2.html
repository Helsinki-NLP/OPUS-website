Task 4.2 Agreements with text providers (D4.2)

Activities in this task are to (i) provide a survey of available corpora and formats, including information about used domain classification systems, (ii) to produce sample data or descriptions of how to acquire the data, (iii) make agreements with text providers; agreements must include information about the amount of data made available for the project, and (iv) to collect sample data, and (v) to explore the possibilities for continuous collection of texts throughout the project period. In this task there will be collaboration with WP2 about allowed formats etc.

(FFZG, UUP, TILDE, UCPH, MOR, SEM)
