Task 7.1 Dissemination activities (D7.1, D7.4, D7.5)

Dissemination of information about the project, its goals and results is planned via miscellaneous channels such as leaflets, posters, press releases, scientific publications, partners’ websites, LetsMT! website, conferences, seminars and workshops along with events organized by industry associations (TAUS, LISA). An initial dissemination plan (D7.1/M3) will be created to identify the most appropriate channels, means, events and activities for popularizing the LetsMT! services.

(TILDE, UEDIN, FFZG, UCPH, UUP, SEM, MOR)
