Task 2.4 Populating SMT Resource Repository with initial resources (D2.3)

Training resources of different kind for different languages and different domains collected in Task 4.3/D4.3 will be prepared and uploaded to SMT Resource Repository to test functionality of repository (Task 2.1), upload mechanisms (Task 2.2) and data processing and evaluation mechanisms (Task 2.3). Uploaded resources will be processed, validated and evaluated.

(UUP, UCPH, TILDE, FFZG, SEM, MOR)
