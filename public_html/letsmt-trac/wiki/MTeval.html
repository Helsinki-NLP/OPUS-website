Tools & Metrics for the Evaluation of MT

  • NIST BLEU ​ftp://jaguar.ncsl.nist.gov/mt/resources/mteval-v11b.pl
  • METOR: ​http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~alavie/METEOR/
  • The Asiya Open Toolkit for Automatic MT (Meta-)Evaluation
    ​http://www.lsi.upc.edu/~nlp/Asiya/
  • TER: ​http://www.umiacs.umd.edu/~snover/terp/
  • ​http://sourceforge.net/projects/tercpp
  • really nice with different metrics & significance testing:
    • ​https://github.com/jhclark/multeval
  • also combining various metrics in a simple script:
    • ​http://kheafield.com/code/scoring.tar.gz
  • visualization: ​https://github.com/mjdenkowski/meteor/tree/master/xray
  • in Moses: evaluator --sctype TER|BLEU|... --reference ref.file --candidate cand.file
