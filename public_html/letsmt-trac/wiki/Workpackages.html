LetsMT Workpackages

Here is a short summary of our workpackages:

LetsMT! platform and infrastructure (WP1)

  • Period: M1 - M25 (65 pm, UUP: 12)
  • Leading partner: TILDE

The WP1 is the central work package in which a collaborative LetsMT! platform will be designed, integrated, deployed, tested, and elaborated (Figure B.3.2.a.3). Requirement analysis and design of the LetsMT! platform and its constituents will be created within the scope of this work package. Supporting hardware and software infrastructure will be designed, implemented and deployed. The Web page for choosing the SMT system, typing or pasting texts and obtaining translation will also be implemented. The LetsMT! constituents developed through WP2-WP5 will be integrated into the platform, deployed and tested.

SMT resource repository and data processing facilities (WP2)

  • Period: M2 - M18 (58 pm, UUP: 21)
  • Leading partner: UUP

LetsMT! will create a SMT Resource repository where users can store and share their data and search for public data of other users. Data upload mechanisms and tools for processing uploaded content will be developed. Initial SMT training resources from partners will be collected and incorporated into the Repository. Information about resources available through the LetsMT! platform for MT training will be stored in SMT Resource Directory. The SMT Resource Directory will also provide functionality necessary for site administrators to manage SMT Resource Repository.

SMT training facilities and SMT web service (WP3)

  • Period: M1 - M18 (55 pm, UUP: 8)
  • Leading partner: UEDIN

The main objective of the LetsMT! project is to respond to users’ need for user-data specific SMT systems. Thus WP3 will create facilities for training of SMT systems on training data selected and/or provided by user. The Web service interface for 24/11/2009 version: 4.0 250456 LetsMT! 54 accessing trained SMT systems will be created. Moses toolkit will be adapted to fit to rapid training / updating / interactive access environment. Initial SMT systems for pilot applications will be trained in this work package.

Collecting training data (WP4)

  • Period: M1 - M30 (89 pm, UUP: 13)
  • Leading partner: UCPH

The aim of this work package is to collect data (corpora) in general domain, business and financial news domain for WP5 and localization domain for WP6. The data will be collected from various providers, based on agreements with them, LetsMT! partners and cooperation partners such as TAUS DA. The collected data will be stored in the repository (WP2) using the facilities of the repository, and the available corpora from partners will be used to train the SMT systems available in the LetsMT! platform.

MT usage in news translation: facilities and evaluation (WP5)

  • Period: M7 - M30 (39 pm)
  • Leading partner: SEM

WP5 aims to provide access to the LetsMT! platform to general users for business and financial news translation through two facilities: the LetsMT! translation widget and the web browser plug-in for web page translation. Data necessary for business and financial news translation systems will be collected in WP4 and corresponding SMT systems will be trained in WP5. The general usage SMT systems and facilities will be evaluated and elaborated iteratively in this WP..

MT usage in localisation: facilities and evaluation (WP6)

  • Period: M1 - M30 (36 pm)
  • Leading partner: MOR

WP6 aims to provide access to the LetsMT! platform to professional users using CAT tools for localisation. Data necessary for domain specific SMT systems used in localisation will be collected and corresponding SMT systems trained. SMT systems and facilities will be evaluated and elaborated iteratively. Both internal (partners) and external users (end users) along with LetsMT! Support Group will be involved in evaluation. The system will have interfaces through which professional translators will access the system from their CAT tools.

Dissemination and sustainability (WP7)

  • Period: M1 - M30 (38 pm, UUP: 7)
  • Leading partner: FFZG

WP7 will be dedicated to (i) dissemination of information about the project, (ii) to ensuring the sustainability of LetsMT! service and (iii) to providing the necessary legal framework that will deal with IPR. Market analysis and financial planning will be conducted to create a business plan. The dissemination plan will identify the appropriate channels, events and activities for popularizing the service. A public website will be maintained and presentation materials produced. Contract forms for different types of content providers and other users will be developed.

Management (WP8)

  • Period: M0 - M30 (33 pm, UUP: 1)
  • Leading partner: TILDE

The objectives of WP8 are: (i) ensuring project compliance with EC rules, (ii) meeting the objectives of the project within the agreed budget and timeframe, (iii) coordinating project activities and ensuring effective internal communication, (iv) carrying out quality control of the work performed and of deliverables, (v) providing adequate information to decision making bodies, (vi) informing governing bodies about problems or conflict situations and (vii) setting up an organisation to support the above objectives.
